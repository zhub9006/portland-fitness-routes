## Description

A challenging out-and-back cycling route starting from central Forest Park on Leif Erikson Drive, climbing northward to the ridge at the top of the park, then descending via NW Germantown Road for a longer, varied return. This route features the signature climb of the Forest Park cycling network — a steady ascent through old-growth forest with expanding city views as you gain elevation. The descent on Germantown Road is fast, technical in sections, and offers a completely different riding experience from the climb. Advanced riders can extend the route by continuing to the Saltzman Road connector for a ~15 km loop.

## Turn-by-Turn Directions

1. **Start** — Northwest Leif Erikson Drive near NW Saint Helens Road (45.5594, -122.7737)
2. **Head north** on Leif Erikson Drive — begin the gradual climb through the forest
3. **Continue** for ~400 m on Leif Erikson Drive to NW Bridge Avenue intersection
4. **Continue** north, now climbing more steeply — ~1.8 km to the NW Germantown Road intersection
5. **Turn right (west)** on NW Germantown Road — fast descent begins
6. **Descend** through the switchback section of Germantown Road — ~1.7 km
7. **Continue** west on Germantown Road, now following a strong downhill with city views to the north — ~2.9 km
8. **Turn left (south)** on NW Bridge Avenue — ~433 m
9. **Turn left (east)** on NW Saint Helens Road — rejoin the forest corridor
10. **Continue** south on Saint Helens Road back toward central Forest Park — ~1.8 km
11. **End** back at the starting point on Leif Erikson Drive

### Extension Option

From the Germantown Road / Bridge Avenue junction, continue west to NW Saltzman Road (~1.2 km further) for a longer loop (~15 km total).

## Tips

- Best attempted after warming up — the climb demands leg power
- Bring water — there are no fountains on this route
- Check for automotive traffic on Germantown Road — it's a busy neighborhood street
