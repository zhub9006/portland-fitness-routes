## Description

A gentle, family-friendly walking trail through the lower elevations of Forest Park. Perfect for club members recovering from injury, beginners, or anyone who wants to enjoy the forest without a big challenge. The trail passes through old-growth cedar groves and alongside seasonal streams.

## Turn-by-Turn Directions

1. **Start** — Lower Forest Park trailhead (NW 25th Ave & NW Germaine St)
2. **Head east** on the Lower Trail (gravel, well-maintained)
3. **Follow** the trail as it curves through cedar groves
4. **Cross** the seasonal stream bridge
5. **Continue** on the marked path
6. **Loop back** to the starting point

## Amenities

- Parking at NW 25th Ave trailhead
- Restrooms nearby at the visitor center
- Benches along the lower trail section

## Tips

- Great for post-workout cooldowns
- Dog-friendly (leash required)
- Accessible for strollers and wheelchairs on the lower section
