## Description

The ultimate Portland fitness challenge! Start from the waterfront near the Playground area, climb through NW Portland streets, cross the historic Saint Johns Bridge, and finish at Cathedral Park in Forest Park. This route tests endurance and leg strength — save it for your big training days.

## Turn-by-Turn Directions (Bike)

1. **Start** — Waterfront Playground area (Niosi Ter & Naito Pkwy)
2. **Head north** on Naito Pkwy
3. **Turn right** onto NW 15th Ave
4. **Continue** onto NW Thurman St (long climb begins)
5. **Turn left** onto NW 29th Ave (sustained gradient)
6. **Turn right** onto NW Tangier St
7. **Continue** onto NW Saint Helens Rd
8. **Turn left** onto NW Bridge Ave
9. **Cross** the Saint Johns Bridge
10. **Turn right** onto N Philadelphia Ave
11. **Continue** on N Willamette Blvd
12. **Turn left** onto N Baltimore Ave
13. **Arrive** at Cathedral Park

## Turn-by-Turn Directions (Run/Walk)

1. **Start** — Waterfront Playground area (Niosi Ter & Naito Pkwy)
2. **Head north** on Naito Pkwy
3. **Turn right** onto NW 15th Ave
4. **Continue** onto NW Thurman St
5. **Turn left** onto NW 29th Ave (climb at running pace)
6. **Turn right** onto NW Tangier St
7. **Continue** onto NW Saint Helens Rd
8. **Turn left** onto NW Bridge Ave
9. **Cross** the Saint Johns Bridge
10. **Turn right** onto N Philadelphia Ave
11. **Continue** on N Willamette Blvd
12. **Turn left** onto N Baltimore Ave
13. **Arrive** at Cathedral Park

## Tips

- This is a big climb — pace yourself and don't burn out early
- The waterfront start gives you a nice warm-up before the big ascent
- Cathedral Park has restrooms and water for post-ride recovery
- Best for experienced club members — consider supporting slower riders
