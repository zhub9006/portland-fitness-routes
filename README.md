## Route Summary by Type

### Running рҹҸғ
- **Wildwood Trail Loop** вҖ” ~6.0 mi / ~550 ft elevation gain
  - Full loop through the heart of Forest Park via the iconic Wildwood Trail
  - Old-growth Douglas fir, sword ferns, seasonal creek crossings
  - Moderate difficulty; suitable for intermediate runners
  - **OSM Verified:** Lower Macleay вҶ’ Witch's Castle (3,712 m / ~7 min walking); Lower Macleay вҶ’ Pittock Mansion (4,923 m / ~10 min walking)

### Walking рҹҡ¶
- **Leif Erikson Drive** вҖ” 2.1 mi one way (4.2 mi out-and-back)
  - Car-free loop road perfect for walking, jogging, or rolling
  - Shaded canopy tunnel in summer; stunning fall colors
  - **OSM Verified:** One-way: 2,129 m (~5 min); Full loop: 10,403 m (~17 min walking)
- **NW Saint Helens Road** вҖ” 3.3 mi one way
  - Historic road connecting Washington Park to Forest Park
  - Mix of residential and park trail environments
  - **OSM Verified:** 2,896 m one way (via Bridge Ave + St. Helens Rd)
- **City Center вҶ’ Forest Park Walk** вҖ” 2.7 km (~5 min)
  - **OSM Verified:** 2,749 m (via NW 19th Ave + NW Flanders St)

### Cycling рҹҡҙ
- **Skyline Cycling Route** вҖ” ~12.2 mi / ~800 ft elevation
  - NW Skyline Boulevard ridgeline ride with Mt. Hood vistas
  - **OSM Verified:** Extended Leif Erikson routing (~11,622 m)
- **Forest Park to Downtown** вҖ” 14.6 mi / ~900 ft descent
  - Descend through Forest Park into downtown Portland
  - Includes Willamette River waterfront finish
  - **OSM Verified:** Forest Park center вҶ’ Downtown (9,440 m / ~12 min biking); Downtown вҶ’ Forest Park (6,544 m / ~11 min biking)
- **Saltzman Warmup Loop** вҖ” 2.1 mi
  - Tight, fast loop near the north entrance
  - Great for speed work and track-style intervals
  - **OSM Verified:** Saltzman + St. Helens + Bridge Ave (4,586 m)
- **Ridge Loop** вҖ” 4.6 mi
  - Germantown Road вҶ’ Kaiser Road вҶ’ Laidlaw Road circuit
  - **OSM Verified:** Full ridge loop (4,586 m / ~7 min biking)
- **St. Johns Connector** вҖ” 15.0 mi
  - St. Johns Bridge descent into Forest Park
  - **OSM Verified:** St. Johns to Forest Park (15,028 m / ~21 min biking)

## Contributing

To add a new route:
1. Run the route using OSM routing (OsmAnd, GraphHopper, or OSRM)
2. Record distance, duration, and elevation data
3. Create a new file in `routes/` with the naming convention `[route-name].md`
4. Include: Overview table, description, key landmarks, coordinates, and seasonal notes
5. Open a Pull Request
