### Walking рҹҡ¶
- **Leif Erikson Drive** вҖ” 2.7 mi one way (5.4 mi out-and-back)
  - Car-free loop road perfect for walking, jogging, or rolling
  - Shaded canopy tunnel in summer; stunning fall colors
  - **OSM Verified:** 4,390 m one-way (~5.7 min)
- **NW Saint Helens Road** вҖ” 1.8 mi one way
  - Historic road connecting Washington Park to Forest Park
  - Mix of residential and park trail environments
  - **OSM Verified:** 2,896 m one-way
- **City Center вҶ’ Forest Park Walk** вҖ” ~11.4 km
  - **OSM Verified:** 11,428 m (~16.8 min walking)
- **Leif Erikson Drive Full Loop** вҖ” ~10.4 km
  - Car-free on Sundays for a complete loop
  - **OSM Verified:** 10,403 m loop
- **Forest Park вҶ’ Downtown Walking** вҖ” ~4,952 m (short) / ~12,013 m (extended)
  - Walk from Forest Park through residential NW neighborhoods to downtown/river
  - **OSM Verified:** 4,952 m (~8 min walking); 12,013 m (~17 min walking)
- **Forest Park Perimeter Walk** вҖ” ~11,400 m (quadrant) / ~14,800 m (full loop)
  - Full perimeter loop via Cornell Rd, Skyline Blvd, Germantown, St. Helens, Yeon, Going, Channels
  - **OSM Verified:** ~14,800 m (~2.5 hrs)
- **Skyline Boulevard Walk** вҖ” ~11,622 m full loop
  - Forest Park wrap-around via Cornell Rd вҶ’ Skyline Blvd вҶ’ residential streets
  - **OSM Verified:** ~11,622 m (~3 hrs)

### Cycling рҹҡҙ
