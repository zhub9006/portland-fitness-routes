# Forest Park Perimeter Walk

**Route Type:** Walking / **Distance:** ~11,400 m (short quadrant) or ~14,800 m (full loop) / **Elevation:** ~500 ft / **Format:** Loop

## Turn-by-Turn Directions (Short Quadrant — NW Section)

1. Start at **Leif Erikson Dr & Skidmore St** (45.571, -122.772)
2. Head east on **NW Saint Helens Road** (1,445 m)
3. Turn right onto **NW 63rd Avenue** (66 m)
4. Continue onto **NW Bridge Avenue** (174 m)
5. Merge onto **NW Germantown Road** heading west (3,356 m)
6. Continue onto **NW Skyline Boulevard** (4,529 m) — ridgeline panoramic section
7. Follow **NW Thunder Crest Drive** (283 m)
8. Return via **NW Marquam Trail** connector to complete the quadrant
