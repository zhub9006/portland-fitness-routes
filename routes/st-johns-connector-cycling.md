# St. Johns Connector Cycling

**Route Type:** Cycling  
**Distance:** 14,749 m (9.2 mi) — St. Johns to Forest Park  
**Elevation Gain:** ~1,200 ft (descent to park)  
**Duration:** ~22 min ride  

## Overview

A thrilling descent from St. Johns into Forest Park via the St. Johns Bridge approach. The route drops steeply from the bridge area down NW Saint Helens Road into the park. The return ride back up provides a great workout.

## Turn-by-Turn Directions

1. Start at **St. Johns Bridge** (45.593, -122.747) — top of bridge approach
2. Follow **NW Bridge Avenue** south (945 m)
3. Continue on **NW Saint Helens Road** through Forest Park (3,263 m + 2,446 m)
4. Pass **Forest Park Field House** on the left
5. Continue on **NW Nicolai Street**, **NW Wardway Street** (666 m)
6. Arrive at **NW 25th Avenue** (312 m)
7. Turn left on **NW 25th Avenue** heading south
8. Connect to **NW Lovejoy Street** (138 m)
9. Arrive at **Forest Park center** (45.535, -122.738)
