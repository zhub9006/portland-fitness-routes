# St. Johns Connector Cycling Route

**Type:** Cycling | **Category:** Connector / Descent Ride

## Route: St. Johns Bridge to Forest Park
- **Verified Distance:** 14,749 m (~9.2 mi)
- **Duration:** ~22 min bike
- **Elevation Gain:** ~900 ft descent from bridge
- **Surface:** Paved roads + bridge
- **OSM Verified:** Via NW Bridge Avenue, Saint Johns Bridge, NW Philadelphia Ave, NW Ivanhoe St, NW Richmond Ave

### Turn-by-Turn
1. Start at St. Johns Bridge (45.593, -122.747)
2. Cross the St. Johns Bridge
3. Descend onto NW Bridge Avenue
4. Follow NW Bridge Avenue into Forest Park
5. Connect to NW Germantown Road
6. Continue to Forest Park center

### Highlights
- St. Johns Bridge descent into Forest Park (~900 ft drop)
- Scenic New Columbia neighborhood view from the bridge
- Fast, flowing descent
- Connects north Portland to Forest Park
- Moderate climb back if returning the same way
