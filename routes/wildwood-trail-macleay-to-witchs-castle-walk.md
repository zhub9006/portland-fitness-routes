# Wildwood Trail: Macleay to Witch's Castle 🌿⛷

A beautiful walk through old-growth forest along the Wildwood Trail from Lower Macleay Park to the Stone House ruins (Witch's Castle).

## Turn-by-Turn Directions

1. Start at Lower Macleay Trail parking area, NW 25th Ave & Thurman St
2. Follow **NW 31st Avenue** south from parking area (109 m)
3. Turn right onto **NW Thurman Street** heading southeast (853 m)
4. Turn left onto **NW 25th Avenue** heading southwest (392 m)
5. Turn left onto **NW 25th Avenue** continuing through the park (150 m)
6. Continue on unmarked path segment (14 m)
7. Continue on **NW 25th Avenue** (75 m)
8. Turn left onto **NW Lovejoy Street** heading west (138 m)
9. Turn left onto **NW Cornell Road** heading south — this is the Wildwood Trail entrance (1,977 m)
10. Follow Wildwood Trail through old-growth forest, crossing seasonal creeks
11. Trail ends at the Stone House ruins (Witch's Castle)

---

## Highlights

- 🌲 **Old-growth forest** — towering Douglas firs, western red cedars, and bigleaf maples
- 🌿 **Moss-covered logs** and fern-lined footbridges
- 🏚️ **Witch's Castle (Stone House)** — hauntingly beautiful ruins from the 1930s
- 🌊 **Seasonal creek crossings** — may be impassable after heavy rain
- 🦌 **Wildlife** — common to see deer, raccoons, and varied bird species

## Tips

- Bring a flashlight — the Stone House area can be dark
- Check trail conditions after rain — creek crossings may be high
- Best in fall for golden foliage; also beautiful in spring after rainy season
- Hikers have right-of-way on narrow sections
