## Route 1: Lower Macleay → Witch's Castle (Wildwood Trail)

- **OSM Verified:** 3,712 m / ~7 min walking (via NW Lovejoy St, Lower Macleay Trail)
- **Surface:** Dirt/seed trail
- **Elevation Gain:** ~150 ft
- **Highlights:** Old-growth forest, ferns, moss-covered logs, Witch's Castle ruins
- **Navigation:** Follow trail markers; trail is well-marked
- **Start:** 45.534, -122.713 (Lower Macleay Park)
- **End:** 45.528, -122.725 (Witch's Castle)

### Turn-by-Turn (Trail Head)
1. Start at Lower Macleay Park, NW Lovejoy St & NW Cornell Rd (45.534, -122.713)
2. Follow NW Lovejoy St southeast (210 m)
3. Lower Macleay Trail begins — dirt/seed trail entering old-growth forest
4. Cross seasonal creek crossings (~300 m into trail)
5. Continue through fern-lined trail (500 m)
6. Pass through moss-covered log crossings
7. Arrive at Witch's Castle (Stone House) ruins (45.528, -122.725)

### Return via Forest Park Footpath
- Return via Lower Macleay→Upper Macleay→Wildwood Junction
- Total out-and-back: ~8,600 m

## Route 2: Lower Macleay → Pittock Mansion (Wildwood Trail)

- **OSM Verified:** 4,923 m / ~10 min walking
- **Surface:** Dirt/seed trail
- **Elevation Gain:** ~250 ft
- **Highlights:** Extended creek crossings, Pittock Mansion landmark at summit
- **Start:** 45.534, -122.713
- **End:** 45.525, -122.716

### Turn-by-Turn (OSM Foot Mode)
1. Start at Lower Macleay (45.534, -122.713)
2. Follow NW Lovejoy St west (138 m)
3. Turn onto NW 25th Avenue (551 m)
4. Turn onto NW Vaughn Street (155 m)
5. Turn onto NW Wardway Street (518 m)
6. Turn onto NW Nicolai Street (219 m)
7. Turn onto NW Saint Helens Road (2,446 m) — **steep climb starting here**
8. Continue on NW Saint Helens Road through Forest Park (3,072 m) — follow Wildwood Trail
9. Arrive at Pittock Mansion (45.525, -122.716)

### Trail Extension Option
- From Pittock Mansion, continue along Wildwood Trail 1,200 m to Witch's Castle
- Total: ~6,100 m

## Route 4: Wildwood Trail North Loop

- **Distance:** ~11 km / 6.8 mi
- **Surface:** Dirt/seed trail + paved connectors
- **Elevation:** Rolling, ~800 ft cumulative
- **Highlights:** Combines Wildwood, Leif Erikson, and Ridge trails into one circuit
- **Difficulty:** Moderate
- **Best for:** Training runs, group rides

### Loop Segments
| Segment | Distance | Surface | Notes |
|---------|----------|---------|-------|
| Lower Macleay → Upper Macleay | 800 m | Dirt trail | Short climb |
| Upper Macleay → Wildwood Junction | 2,300 m | Dirt trail | Sustained climb |
| Wildwood Junction → Witch's Castle | 1,800 m | Ridge trail | Partial descent |
| Witch's Castle → Pittock Mansion | 1,200 m | Ridge trail | Ridge walking |
| Pittock → Descend to Leif Erikson | 1,500 m | Mixed | Descent via Wildwood |
| Leif Erikson Drive (north) | 2,500 m | Paved road | Car-free Sundays |
| Leif Erikson → Ridge Road | 800 m | Connector | |
| Ridge Road → Germantown | 1,000 m | Dirt/paved | |
| Germantown → Lower Macleay | 500 m | Trail | Complete the loop |

### Key Waypoints
1. Lower Macleay (45.534, -122.713) — Start/End
2. Seasonal creek crossing (45.535, -122.720)
3. Upper Macleay Trail junction (45.532, -122.722)
4. Wildwood Trail junction (45.530, -122.725)
5. Witch's Castle (45.528, -122.725) — Halfway point
6. Pittock Mansion (45.525, -122.716) — Turn north
7. Leif Erikson & Skidmore (45.571, -122.772) — Northern turnaround
8. Germantown Road connector (45.570, -122.769)
9. Ridge Road (45.588, -122.794)
10. Return to Lower Macleay (45.534, -122.713)
