# Leif Erikson Drive Full Length Cycle

## Route Overview
- **Distance:** ~11 miles (one way)
- **Elevation Gain:** ~1,100 ft (northward)
- **Terrain:** Paved, car-free
- **Difficulty:** 🟢 Easy-Moderate
- **Start:** Burnside St / NW 26th Ave
- **End:** Gorum Ridge area
- **GPS Coordinates:** Start 45.5709, -122.7181 → End ~45.525, -122.720

## Description
The full length of Leif Erikson Drive from Burnside to the park's northern boundary near Gorum Ridge. The road is closed to cars on weekdays and all day on Sundays, making it ideal for cycling.

## Turn-by-Turn Directions
1. Start at Burnside St / NW 26th Ave
2. Head north on Leif Erikson Drive (paved, car-free)
3. Pass NW Thurman St intersection at ~1 mile
4. Continue through the forested corridor alongside Balch Creek
5. Pass Wildwood Trail junction at ~2 miles
6. Continue north through Hoyt Arboretum area
7. Pass NW Skyline Blvd junction at ~4 miles
8. Continue on Leif Erikson Drive as it climbs gently
9. Pass NH Middle Creek Road junction at ~7 miles
10. Continue to the northern terminus near Gorum Ridge at ~11 miles
11. Turn around and return to the start

## Best For
- Road cycling
- Long rides
- Family rides

## Notes
- Car-free Weekdays (dawn to ~10am) and all day Sundays
- Popular with runners too — share the road!
- Significant northward climb — plan accordingly for the return
- Bring water and snacks for the full distance
- Sunday mornings (7am–12pm) are peak club ride time!
