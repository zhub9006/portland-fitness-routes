# Forest Park Perimeter Walking Loop 🏞️

A full perimeter loop around Forest Park, combining forest trails, ridge roads, and neighborhood streets for a comprehensive walk that showcases every side of the park.

**OSM Verified:** ~11,400 m (short quadrant) | ~14,800 m (full loop) | **Duration:** ~2.5 hrs (full loop)

---

## Route Details

| Field | Value |

## Route Segments

### Segment 1: Ridge Trail to Wildwood Trail (NW Germantown Rd)
- **Distance:** ~2,000 m
- **Route:** Follow NW Germantown Road west from the Field House toward the Wildwood Trail area
- **Surface:** Paved road

### Segment 2: Wildwood Trail (NW Germantown to Skyline Blvd)
- **Distance:** ~3,000 m
- **Route:** Enter Wildwood Trail from NW Germantown, follow trail north toward Skyline Blvd
- **Surface:** Dirt/seed trail

### Segment 3: Skyline Boulevard (NW)
- **Distance:** ~3,500 m
- **Route:** Follow NW Skyline Boulevard west along the ridgeline toward Forest Park's northwest boundary
- **Surface:** Paved road

### Segment 4: Ridge Trail (NW Quadrant)
- **Distance:** ~2,000 m
- **Route:** Descend via Ridge Trail toward NW Forest Park, cross through residential connectors
- **Surface:** Trail + sidewalks

### Segment 5: NW Saint Helens Road (East)
- **Distance:** ~2,896 m (one-way)
- **Route:** Follow NW Saint Helens Road from the park east toward Washington Park
- **Surface:** Paved road + sidewalks

### Segment 6: Return via Residential Streets
- **Distance:** ~1,400 m
- **Route:** Connect through NW neighborhoods back to starting point
- **Surface:** Sidewalks, residential streets

---

## Turn-by-Turn Directions

1. Start at **Forest Park Field House** on NW Germantown Rd
2. Head west on **NW Germantown Rd** (paved, 2 km to Wildwood Trail area)
3. Turn left onto **Wildwood Trail** heading northwest (3 km)
4. Turn right onto **NW Skyline Blvd** heading west along the ridge (3.5 km)
5. Continue past Cornell Road, stay on Skyline Blvd (1.5 km)
6. Descend via **Ridge Trail** toward NW quadrant (2 km)
7. Connect to **NW 53rd Ave** heading south (800 m)
8. Turn left onto **NW Lovejoy St** heading east (1.2 km)
9. Turn left onto **NW 25th Ave** heading south (500 m)
10. Turn right onto **NW Thurman St** heading east (800 m)
11. Return to **Forest Park Field House** via NW Germantown Rd
