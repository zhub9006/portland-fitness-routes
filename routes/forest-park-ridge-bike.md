# Route: Forest Park Ridge Cycling Route

- **Type:** Cycling
- **Distance:** 10,225 meters (~6.4 miles)
- **Duration:** ~30 minutes cycling
- **Difficulty:** Moderate (significant hills)
- **Start:** NW Willalatin Road area (45.581, -122.792)
- **End:** NW Yeon Avenue area (45.547, -122.716)
- **Source:** OpenStreetMap routing analysis

## Description
A challenging cycling route climbing from the NW Willalatin Road area through the Forest Park ridge, following Skyline Boulevard and Germantown Road. This route tests endurance with sustained climbs and offers rewarding views of Portland and the surrounding mountains at the top. Best suited for experienced club riders.

## Turn-by-Turn Directions (from OSM)
1. **Start** at NW Willalatin Road (45.581, -122.792)
2. Brief segment on **NW Willalatin Road** — 117 m
3. Turn onto **NW Skyline Boulevard** — 437 m
4. Continue onto **NW Germantown Road** — 3,356 m (main climb!)
5. Cross **NW Bridge Avenue** — 433 m
6. Continue on **NW Bridge Avenue** — 990 m
7. Merge onto **NW Saint Helens Road** — 3,263 m
8. End at **NW Yeon Avenue** — 1,352 m

## Key Features
- NW Germantown Road: the premier climbing segment in Portland
- Forest canopy shading for most of the route
- Views of Mt. Hood and the Cascades from upper Germantown
- Connects to the Forest Park trail network at multiple points
- Seismic upgrade zone — watch for road work dates

## Facilities Nearby
- **Parking:** NW Skyline Blvd & NW Germantown Road pullout
- **Bicycle parking:** World Forestry Center (NW 29th Ave & Skyline)
- **Restrooms:** Oregon Zoo area (south end of route)

## Safety Notes
- NW Germantown Road has steep drop-offs — stay to the right
- Motor vehicle traffic on all road segments — use lights and reflectors
- Best ride time: weekday mornings (less traffic)
- Avoid during heavy rain — Germantown Road can be slippery
