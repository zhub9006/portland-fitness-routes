# NW Saint Helens to Ridge Cycling

**Route Type:** Cycling  
**Distance:** 4,585 m (2.8 mi) short / ~14,310 m (8.9 mi) extended loop  
**Elevation Gain:** ~600 ft  
**Duration:** ~8 min short; ~25 min extended loop  

## Overview

A versatile cycling route starting on NW Saint Helens Road, descending into Forest Park, then climbing via Germantown Road to the ridge for panoramic views. The extended version loops back via Yeon Road and connecting streets for a full 14+ km ride.

## Turn-by-Turn Directions (Short: St. Helens Descent + Ridge Ascent)

1. Start at **NW Bridge Avenue** (45.577, -122.761)
2. Follow **NW Saint Helens Road** south (3,263 m)
3. Bear right onto **NW Nicolai Street** (219 m)
4. Follow **NW Wardway Street** and connector roads (447 m)
5. Climb on **NW Germantown Rd** west (1,320 m)
6. Extend via **NW Ridge Avenue** (1,320 m)

## Extended Loop (via Yeon Road)

1. From the ridge, continue on **NW Yeon Road** north (2,400 m)
2. Turn right on **NW Cornell Road** heading south (1,500 m)
3. Return to start at NW Bridge Avenue
