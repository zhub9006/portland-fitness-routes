# NW Saint Helens to Ridge Cycling Route 🚴

**Route Type:** Road Cycling | **Difficulty:** Moderate | **Elevation:** ~500 ft

---

## Route Overview

A connectivity ride from the Fig Portland / St. Johns area into the heart of Forest Park via the Ridgeline. This route combines a scenic descent on NW Saint Helens Road with a rolling climb up Germantown Road to the Ridge trail network. Perfect for a warm-up or lead-in to longer Wildwood Trail runs.

## Turn-by-Turn Directions

1. Head north on **NW Saint Helens Road** → 1,777 m
   - Well-paved, moderate traffic, beautiful tree-lined road
   - Cross under the **St. Johns Bridge** approach for a dramatic entry
2. Turn right onto **NW Bridge Avenue** → 174 m
   - Short connector with views of the bridge towers
3. Turn left onto **NW Germantown Road** → 2,487 m
   - 🔥 **Key climb:** Gradual but steady ascent into the Forest Park ridgeline
   - Switches through the forest canopy with moss-covered maples
   - Watch for trail connections on your right (Wildwood, Ridge, etc.)
4. Arrive at **Ridge Trail Parking Lot** / Germantown Ridge junction
   - **End point:** (45.588, -122.794)

- 🏔️ **Ridge panorama** — Elevated views of Columbia River Gorge and Mt. Hood from Kaiser/Laidlaw

## How to Use This Route

### Option 1: Short Ride (15 min)
1. Start at **NW Saint Helens & Leif Erikson** (45.571, -122.772)
2. Ride north on **NW Saint Helens Road** → 1,777 m
3. Turn onto **NW Bridge Avenue** → 174 m
4. Climb up **NW Germantown Road** → 2,487 m
5. Arrive at **Ridge Trail Parking Lot** (45.588, -122.794)
6. Turn around and retrace

### Option 2: Ridge Loop (25 min)
1. Follow the short ride to the Ridge
2. At the Ridge, take **Kaiser Road → Laidlaw Road** back toward St. Helens
3. Return to St. Helens and reverse the route

### Option 3: Full Extended Loop (45 min)
1. Follow the extended loop route detailed above
2. End at **North Ensign Street** / downtown area
3. Restock at a downtown cafe before heading back

## Safety Notes

- ⚠️ **Germantown Road climb** — Share the road with runners; announce your presence with a bell
- ⚠️ **Yeon Avenue descent** — Motorists speed; use caution on the fast sections
- ⚠️ **Crosswinds** — The ridge and bridge approaches can be windy; secure loose clothing
- 🦴 **Dogs on leashes** — Common on all sections; pass carefully
- 🚦 **Traffic lights** — Some signals on the descent segments; plan accordingly
