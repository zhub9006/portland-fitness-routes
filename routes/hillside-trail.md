# Hillside Trail to Pittock Mansion

**Route Type:** Hiking / Running  
**Distance:** ~4.0 miles (6.4 km) round trip  
**Elevation Gain:** ~600 ft  
**Difficulty:** Moderate  
**Surface:** Dirt trail, some gravel  
**Best Season:** Spring through Fall  

---

## Overview

The Hillside Trail rises from the northern end of the Wildwood Trail, climbing through the forest to a ridge with expansive city views. This route connects to the Wildwood Trail and finishes at the iconic Pittock Mansion — a favorite Forest Park destination.

## Route Details

### Out-and-Back
- **Distance:** ~4.0 mi (2.0 mi each way) from Pittock
- **Duration:** ~45 min running / ~1 hr 15 min hiking
- **Start:** Pittock Mansion (45.525, -122.716)
- **End:** Hillside Trail / Wildwood Trail junction (45.524, -122.716)

### Turn-by-Turn
1. **Start at Pittock Mansion** (45.5252, -122.7162)
2. Walk/bike west on **NW Pittock Drive** (543 m)
3. Connect to **NW Pittock Avenue** (280 m)
4. Take **NW Tanner Creek Road** or **NW Highland Road**
5. Join **Hillside Trail** heading south
6. Follow Hillside Trail through the forest
7. Connect to **Wildwood Trail main** (45.5236, -122.7184)
8. Continue east to **WNW 24th Place** or complete loop

### Key Landmarks
- **Pittock Mansion** — Historic mansion with panoramic views
- **Hillside Trail connector** — Steep forest trail segment
- **Wildwood Trail junction** — Connect to the main park trail network

## Tips

- The Hillside Trail connector is steep — take it slow on the climb
- Pittock Mansion is a great turnaround/rest stop
- Combine with the Wildwood Trail loop for a 10+ mile day
- Best hiking in the morning (afternoon shadows reduce visibility)
- Leashed dogs allowed.
