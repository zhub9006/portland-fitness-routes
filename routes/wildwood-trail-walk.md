# Wildwood Trail Walking Route 🚶

A walking route through Forest Park's most scenic Wildwood Trail section.

## Route 1: Lower Macleay → Witch's Castle (Stone House)

| Detail | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Distance** | 3,379 m (2.1 mi) one way |
| **Elevation gain** | ~500 ft |
| **Duration** | ~40 min walk |
| **Difficulty** | Easy–Moderate |

### Turn-by-Turn Directions (OSM Verified)

1. Start at **Lower Macleay Park** trailhead (45.534, -122.713)
2. Head east on **NW Raleigh Street** — 452 m
3. Continue on **NW 25th Avenue** — 233 m
4. Take **NW Westover Road** east — 373 m
5. Connect to **NW Westside Road** — 483 m
6. Cross **West Burnside Road** — 1,130 m
7. Turn south onto **NW Barnes Road** — 174 m
8. Continue onto **NW Pittock Avenue** — 280 m
9. Connect to **NW Pittock Drive** — 556 m
10. Continue to Wildwood Trail junction — short segment
11. Follow Wildwood Trail north to **Witch's Castle** (45.528, -122.725) — via Old-growth forest

### Highlights
- Old-growth Douglas fir and western red cedar
- Seasonal creek crossings
- Historic Stone House ruins (Witch's Castle)
- Dense fern canopy

| Lower Macleay Park | 45.534 | -122.713 |
| NW 25th Ave & Raleigh | 45.533 | -122.711 |
