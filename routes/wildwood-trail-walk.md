# Wildwood Trail Walking Route

**Type:** Walking | **Category:** Trail

## Route 1: Lower Macleay → Witch's Castle
- **Verified Distance:** 3,379 m (~2.1 mi)
- **Duration:** ~45-55 min walk
- **Elevation Gain:** ~150 ft
- **OSM Verified:** Via NW Lovejoy St, Lower Macleay Trail, Wildwood Trail
- **Surface:** Dirt/seed trail

### Turn-by-Turn
1. Start at Lower Macleay Park (45.534, -122.713)
2. Follow NW Lovejoy St into the park
3. Turn onto Lower Macleay Trail
4. Continue on Wildwood Trail (well-marked)
5. Arrive at Witch's Castle (Stone House) ruins (45.528, -122.725)

### Highlights
- Old-growth forest with towering Douglas firs and western red cedars
- Fern-covered trails and moss-covered logs
- Cascade Creek crossings (seasonal)
- Historic Stone House ruins at the end

### Trail Etiquette
- Hikers have right-of-way on narrow paths
- Ring bell or announce when passing others
- Stay on marked trails

## Route 2: Lower Macleay → Pittock Mansion
- **Verified Distance:** 4,594 m (~2.9 mi)
- **Duration:** ~60-75 min walk
- **Elevation Gain:** ~250 ft
- **OSM Verified:** Via NW 25th Ave, Westover Rd, Pittock Dr, NW Pittock Avenue
- **Surface:** Dirt/seed trail + paved

### Turn-by-Turn
1. Start at Lower Macleay Park (45.534, -122.713)
2. Follow NW 25th Avenue toward the park
3. Take Westover Road into Washington Park
4. Cross Burnside Street
5. Follow NW Barnes Road to Pittock Avenue
6. Continue to NW Pittock Drive
7. Arrive at Pittock Mansion (45.525, -122.716)

### Highlights
- Extended creek crossings through old-growth forest
- Pittock Mansion landmark at summit with panoramic views
- More elevation gain than Route 1
