# Wildwood Trail Walk

**Route Type:** Walking  
**Distance:** 3,379 m (1.1 mi) — Macleay to Witch's Castle  
**Elevation Gain:** ~400 ft  
**Duration:** ~55 min walk  

## Overview

A beautiful walk through old-growth forest on the Wildwood Trail, starting from Lower Macleay Park and heading north to the historic Witch's Castle (Stone House) ruins. This is the perfect easy-to-moderate walk for the whole family, with gentle elevation changes and stunning canopy views.

## Turn-by-Turn Directions

1. Start at **Lower Macleay Park** (45.534, -122.713) — parking and trailhead at NW Cornell Road
2. Follow the **Wildwood Trail** north through old-growth forest (3,379 m)
3. Cross seasonal creek crossings along the trail
4. Arrive at **Witch's Castle (Stone House)** ruins (45.528, -122.725) — historic stone ruins on the trail

## Notes
- Seasonal creek crossings — wear water-resistant shoes in spring
- The Wildwood Trail connects to many other routes in the park
- Leif Erikson Drive is just around the corner for an extended loop
