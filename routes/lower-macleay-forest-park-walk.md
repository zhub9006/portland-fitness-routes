# Lower Macleay to Forest Park Heart Walking Route

## Overview

A beginner-friendly 6.5-mile out-and-back from Lower Macleay deep into the heart of Forest Park.

## Route Details

- **Distance:** 6.5 miles (10.5 km)
- **Difficulty:** Moderate (beginner-friendly with gradual climb)
- **Duration:** ~88 minutes
- **Type:** Out & Back
- **Elevation Gain:** ~600 ft

## Route Description

Starting at **Lower Macleay Trailhead** (45.534, -122.713):

1. **Lower Macleay → Northwest Leif Erikson Drive** (1.2 mi) – Hike up through the fern-lined creek bed.
2. **Northwest Leif Erikson Drive → Lower Macleay Trail → Upper Macleay** (1.5 mi) – Continue on the main access road.
3. **Upper Macleay → Northwest Saint Helens Road** (1.0 mi) – Transition from trail to road.
4. **Northwest Saint Helens Road → Forest Park core** (2.8 mi) – Continue deeper into the park.

## Key Road Sections

| Section | Distance | Road Name |

## Highlights

- 🌿 Historic Upper Macleay Park
- 🌱 Beautiful fern-lined creek beds on the Leif Erikson stretch
- 🌲 Old-growth forest vibes as you go deeper
- 🦌 Wildlife corridor – deer and owl sightings common

## Safety Notes

- ✅ Beginner-friendly – gradual climb
- ⚠️ Limited cell coverage in deep forest sections
- 🥾 Wear hiking shoes for trail sections
- 🦟 Bugs can be heavy May–September
- 🌧️ Muddy conditions Nov–May – waterproof footwear recommended
