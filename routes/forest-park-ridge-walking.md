# Forest Park Ridge Walking Route

**Type:** Walking
**Difficulty:** Moderate
**Best Season:** Spring–Fall

---

## Route: Ridge Trail Walking Loop via NW Germantown Rd, NW Kaiser Rd, NW Bethany Blvd

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Distance** | 8,662 m (~5.4 mi) |
| **Duration** | ~110 min walking |
| **Elevation Gain** | ~600 ft (rolling ridge) |
| **OSM Verified** | ✅ Foot routing via NW Germantown Rd, NW Kaiser Rd, NW Bethany Blvd |

**Coordinates:**
- NW Germantown Rd & Ridge: 45.570, -122.769
- NW Kaiser Rd: 45.575, -122.765
- NW Bethany Blvd: 45.580, -122.755

**Route Segments:**
| Segment | Distance | Road |
|---------|----------|------|
| Ridge trail start → Kaiser Rd connector | ~2,200 m | NW Germantown Rd, NW Ridge trail buffer |
| Kaiser Rd → Bethany Blvd connector | ~3,100 m | NW Kaiser Rd, NW Bethany Blvd |
| Bethany Blvd → Ridge trail return | ~3,362 m | NW Bethany Blvd → NW Ridge roads → NW Germantown Rd |

**Highlights:**
- 🌄 Panoramic ridge views of Portland, Mt. Hood, and the Willamette Valley
- 🌲 Quiet ridge bucolic path with minimal traffic
- 🏃‍♂️ Excellent walking workout with sustained climbing and descending
- 📸 Great photo ops from Kaiser Road and Bethany Blvd viewpoints

**Running Club Notes:**
- The ridge walk pairs perfectly with the Ridge Loop for a combined 13.3 km ride/walk
- Best in fall when the ridge foliage is at peak color
- Not recommended in winter icy conditions on Kaiser Rd
- Bring a light pack with water — there are no facilities on the ridge
- Morning light is best for photos from the ridge viewpoints
