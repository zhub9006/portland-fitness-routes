# Forest Park Ridge Walking Route 🌿

**Route Type:** Walking / Trail Running
**OSM Verified Distance (one-way):** 8,662 m (5.4 miles)
**Round-Trip Distance:** ~17,324 m (10.8 miles)
**Estimated Duration (one-way):** ~790 seconds (~13 minutes)
**Elevation Gain:** Moderate (~300-400 ft)
**Difficulty:** Moderate

## Route Description

A ridge walking route along the northern edge of Forest Park, utilizing NW Germantown Road, Kaiser Road, and Bethany Boulevard. Offers ridge panoramas with potential Mt. Hood views on clear days. Includes a walk across the scenic Saint Johns Bridge.

**End:** Northern Ridge (NW Bethany Boulevard area)

## Route Highlights

- **Saint Johns Bridge walk** — Iconic 1931 bridge
- **Germantown Ridge** — Mt. Hood views on clear days
- Popular for trail running and speed work
- Cooler temps (5-10°F below city)

## Seasonal Notes

- Spring: Germantown Road can be slick after rain
- Summer: Excellent ridge riding conditions
- Fall: Spectacular foliage line
- Winter: Black ice possible on the Germantown hill

## Safety Tips

- Bridge has dedicated pedestrian lanes
- Germantown Road hill is steep on descent
- Less shade on ridge — bring sun protection
- More exposure than the core loop
