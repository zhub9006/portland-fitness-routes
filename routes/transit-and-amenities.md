## ⚠️ Safety Notes

- **No water fountains on trails** — bring your own water
- **Can be muddy after rain** — wear appropriate footwear on dirt trails
- **Shared use** — trails are shared by walkers, runners, and cyclists; announce your presence when passing
- **Dog-friendly** — dogs allowed on most trails but must be leashed in some areas
- **Visibility** — the forest canopy is dense; paths can be dark even during daytime under heavy cover

---

## 🌲 Forest Park Facts

- **Size:** 5,200 acres — one of the largest urban forests in the US
- **Trail length:** Over 80 miles of trails
- **Forest type:** Old-growth Douglas fir, western red cedar, and bigleaf maple
- **Established:** 1948
- **Notable features:** Pittock Mansion, Hoyt Arboretum, Cathedral Park, St. Johns Bridge
