# Lower Macleay to Pittock Mansion Running Loop

**Route Type:** Running  
**Distance:** ~5.5 km (3.4 mi)  
**Elevation Gain:** ~400 ft  
**Difficulty:** Moderate  
**Surface:** Mixed trail, road  
**Best Season:** Year-round  

---

## Overview

A fast, rewarding loop from the Lower Macleay Trailhead through the forest to Pittock Mansion and back via the road. This is a great warmup for longer runs or a standalone tempo run.

## Route Details

### Loop Route
- **Distance:** ~4,923 m (3.0 mi) — out-and-back
- **Duration:** ~20 min running / ~35 min hiking
- **Start:** Lower Macleay Trailhead (45.5344, -122.7132)
- **End:** Pittock Mansion (45.5252, -122.7162)

### Turn-by-Turn (Outbound via Trail)
1. Start at Lower Macleay Trailhead
2. Take **NW Lovejoy St** (63 m)
3. Connect to **Lower Macleay Trail** (begin forest segment)
4. Follow Lower Macleay Trail through old-growth forest (853 m)
5. Transition to **NW Thurman St** (853 m)
6. Join **NW 25th Avenue** (392 m)
7. Merge onto **NW Westover Road** (373 m)
8. Turn onto **NW 24th Place** (176 m)
9. Connect to **West Burnside Street** (483 m)
10. Follow **West Burnside Road** (1,130 m)
11. Turn onto **NW Barnes Road** (174 m)
12. Take **NW Pittock Avenue** (280 m)
13. Arrive at **Pittock Mansion** (45.5252, -122.7162)

### Return Route
- Return via **NW Pittock Drive** (544 m) downhill
- Connect back to **Lower Macleay** via residential streets

## Tips

- The mountain bike-style trail qualifies as a moderate run
- Pittock Mansion has a good bathroom for a post-run pit stop
- Fastest approach: take Pittock Drive downhill and 23rd/24th Place uphill
- Add a Witch's Castle detour for a longer loop (3.7 km one way)
