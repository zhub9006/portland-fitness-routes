# Saltzman Warmup Loop

**Route Type:** Cycling  
**Distance:** 4,586 m (2.9 mi) full loop; 3,764 m (St. Helens segment)  
**Elevation Gain:** ~300 ft  
**Duration:** ~4 min full loop; ~2 min on St. Helens segment  

## Overview

A tight, fast loop near the north entrance of Forest Park, perfect for warm-ups and speed work. The St. Helens Road segment is especially popular for track-style intervals and tempo runs. This route is great for cyclists looking to shake out their legs before a longer ride.

## Turn-by-Turn Directions

1. Start at **NW Bridge Avenue** (45.577, -122.761)
2. Follow **NW Saint Helens Road** (St. Helens segment: 3,764 m round-trip)
3. Bear right onto **NW Nicolai Street** (219 m)
4. Continue to **NW Wardway Street** (447 m)
5. Turn onto **NW 27th Avenue** (79 m)
6. Continue to **NW Upshur Street** (154 m)
7. Loop back via **NW 28th Avenue** (236 m) and **NW Raleigh Street** (302 m)
