## Route Description

A tight, fast loop near the north entrance of Forest Park. Perfect for pre-run warmups, speed work, track-style intervals, and drafting practice. The loop connects via the St. Helens Road/Bridge Avenue system and returns through the residential streets adjacent to the park.

### Loop Direction: Clockwise

1. **Start:** NW Saint Helens Road (midway between Forest Park entrance and Bridge Ave)
2. Head north on **NW Saint Helens Road** (45.535, -122.739)
3. Turn west onto **NW Bridge Avenue** (853.5 m)
4. Cross under **St. Johns Bridge** for dramatic views of the suspension towers
5. Exit onto **North Philadelphia Avenue** heading south
6. Turn south on **North Ivanhoe Street** (323.5 m)
7. Continue on **North Richmond Avenue** (110.5 m)
8. Connect to **North Lombard Street** (125.9 m)
9. Follow **North Oswego Avenue** (452.8 m) back toward the south end
10. Complete the loop via **North Smith Street** connection

### Shorter Warmup: St. Helens Segments Only

For a quick 4-minute warmup:
- **Out:** NW Bridge Ave + NW St. Helens Rd (3,764 m, ~4 min)
- **Return:** Same route or reverse direction
- **Total:** 4,586 m / ~7 min

## Key Landmarks

- **NW Bridge Avenue** — passes under St. Johns Bridge; get a glimpse of the suspension towers
- **North Philadelphia Ave** — residential connector; gentle grade
- **North Ivanhoe Street** — quiet residential street; ideal for high-cadence work
- **North Oswego Avenue** — the return leg; mostly flat

## Interval Training Suggestions

| Drill | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Sprint Intervals** | 30-sec sprints on Bridge Ave (gentle climb), 90-sec recovery |
| **Cadence Work** | High-cadence spinning on Ivanhoe St (flat, quiet) |
| **Drafting Practice** | Paceline on St. Helens Rd (wide shoulder) |
| **Tempo Ride** | Steady moderate pace on full loop, repeat 3-4x |
