# Ridge Loop Cycling Route

**Type:** Cycling  
**Difficulty:** Moderate  
**Best Season:** Spring–Fall

---

## Route: Germantown Road → Kaiser Road → Laidlaw Road Circuit

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Full Loop Distance** | 4,586 m (~2.9 mi) |
| **Duration** | ~7 min biking |
| **Elevation** | ~500 ft (rolling hills) |
| **OSM Verified** | ✅ Bike routing via Wildwood Trail, NW St. Helens Rd, Germantown Rd |

**Coordinates:**
- Wildwood Trail entrance: 45.570, -122.769
- Ridge trail parking: 45.588, -122.794
- Forest Park Field House: 45.583, -122.778

**Route Segments:**
| Segment | Distance | Road |
|---------|----------|------|
| Field House → Ridge Trail Parking | 2,292 m | NW Springville Rd → NW Bridge Ave → NW St. Helens Rd |
| Ridge → Germantown Rd connector | ~1,700 m | Ridge roads |
| Germantown Rd → Kaiser Rd | ~870 m | NW Germantown Rd → NW Kaiser Rd |
| Kaiser Rd → NW Laidlaw Rd | ~900 m | NW Kaiser Rd → NW Laidlaw Rd |
| Laidlaw Rd → Forest Park Center | ~848 m | NW Laidlaw Rd → NW St. Helens Rd |

**Highlights:**
- 🏔️ Germantown Road — steep hill that builds climbing power
- 🌲 Quiet residential connector roads through the ridge
- 📸 Panoramic views from Kaiser Road section
- 🔄 Compact loop for quick bursts of training

**Running Club Tips:**
- Do 3 loops = ~8.7 mi with ~1,500 ft gain
- Sprint reps: hike/bike up Germantown, coast down
- Get off the bike and run the steepest sections
- Winter: can be icy on Germantown — check conditions first
