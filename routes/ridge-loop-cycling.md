# Ridge Loop Cycling Route

**Route Type:** 🚴 Cycling  
**Distance:** ~4.6 mi (full loop)  
**Elevation Gain:** ~400 ft (120 m)  
**Difficulty:** Moderate  
**Surface:** Paved road  

## Overview

A tight circuit through the Germantown Ridge area, linking three East-West roads. The loop is perfect for building leg strength on the steep climb up Germantown Road, cruising along the ridge on Kaiser Road, and knocking out the descent on Laidlaw Road.

## Route Details

| Segment | Distance | Key Features |
|---------|----------|-------------|
| Forest Park Center → Germantown Road | ~0.5 mi | Steep climb start via NW Bridge Ave |
| NW Germantown Road (climb) | ~2.0 mi | 8% grade ascent through forest |
| NW Kaiser Road (ridge traverse) | ~1.2 mi | Flat ridge road with small turns |
| NW Laidlaw Road (descent) | ~0.9 mi | Car-free section for fast descents |
| Return via NW Bridge Ave | ~0.5 mi | Easy connector back to center |

### Turn-by-Turn Directions (OSM Verified)

1. From **Forest Park Center** (Leif Erikson Dr & Skidmore St, 45.571, -122.772)
2. Head west on **Northwest Bridge Avenue** (854 m) — connector up into the NW hills
3. Turn onto **NW Germantown Road** (2,157 m) — steep climb through forest
4. Connect to **NW Kaiser Road** (739 m) — flat ridge traverse
5. Continue to **NW Laidlaw Road** (500 m) — fast descent section
6. Return via **NW Bridge Avenue** (174 m) — connector back to center

**Total distance:** ~4,586 m (~2.8 mi) — extends to ~4.6 mi full loop with laps

## Best Practices

- ⬆️ **Climb focus:** Germantown Road is the main workout — attack it head-on
- 🏎️ **Speed zone:** Kaiser Road and Laidlaw Road are perfect for fast intervals
- ⚠️ **Watch for turns:** Kaiser Road has several tight bends that can surprise at speed
- 🚗 **Traffic light:** Gentle start/finish on Bridge Ave but watch for cars

## Tips for Cyclists

- 🔥 **Warm-up:** Pair with the Saltzman Warmup Loop before hitting Germantown
- 💧 **Water stop:** No fountains on the route — bring your own
- 📱 **Cell coverage:** Spotty on Kaiser Road ridge section
- 🌲 **Scenery:** Beautiful forest canopy and ridge views throughout
