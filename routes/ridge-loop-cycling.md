## Route Description
The Ridge Loop is a scenic circuit through Germantown Ridge, one of the highest points in Forest Park. The route uses quiet residential roads (Kaiser Rd, Laidlaw Rd) and park roads for a smooth, fast loop with panoramic city views.

### Detailed Waypoints
1. **Start:** Wildwood Trail main entrance (45.570, -122.769)
2. **Head west on NW Germantown Rd** through the park
3. **Continue on NW St. Helens Rd** (perpendicular section, ~1,000 m)
4. **Connect to NW Kaiser Rd** (45.575, -122.765)
5. **Continue on NW Laidlaw Rd** (45.573, -122.758)
6. **Loop back** to Wildwood Trail
