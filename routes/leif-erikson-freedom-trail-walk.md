# Leif Erikson Freedom Trail Walk

- **Distance:** ~4.4 mi (7.1 km) out-and-back
- **Elevation Gain:** ~150 ft
- **Estimated Time:** 45–60 min
- **Type:** Out-and-back
- **Terrain:** Paved road, car-free on non-Thursdays
- **Difficulty:** Easy

## Description

A peaceful out-and-back walk along Leif Erikson Drive from the south end (near Forest Park Field House) to the north trailhead at Skidmore Street. The road is closed to vehicles most days, creating a tranquil, car-free walking experience through old-growth forest.

## Route Details

1. **Forest Park Field House** (start)
2. **Leif Erikson Drive** (south to north)
3. **Leif Erikson Dr & Skidmore St** (turnaround)

## OSM Data
- **Leif Erikson Drive** (way): 45.549, -122.734
- **Forest Park Field House** (node): 45.583, -122.778

## Notes
- **Car-free:** Closed to vehicles most days (open Thursdays)
- **Tunnel effect:** Dense tree canopy creates a beautiful green tunnel
- **Fall colors:** Spectacular in late October
- **Extend the walk:** Continue past Skidmore to the Wildwood Trail for a longer route

## Getting There
- **Parking:** Forest Park Field House or Leif Erikson Dr & Skidmore St
- **Transit:** TriMet bus lines 16, 63, 72
