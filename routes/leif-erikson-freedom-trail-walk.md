# Leif Erikson Drive Full Loop (Freedom Trail Walk)

**Route Type:** Walking  
**Distance:** ~10,400 m (6.5 mi) full loop  
**Elevation Gain:** ~500 ft  
**Duration:** ~2.5 hrs  

## Overview

The complete loop around Forest Park using Leif Erikson Drive and connecting forest roads. This is the quintessential walk in Portland's largest urban forest — a 10.4 km circuit through old-growth timber with minimal elevation change. On Sundays, the drive is car-free, making it ideal for walkers.

## Turn-by-Turn Directions

1. Start at **Leif Erikson Dr & Skidmore St** (45.571, -122.772)
2. Head south on **Leif Erikson Drive** through the canopy tunnel
3. At the south end, turn left on **NW Cornell Road** (1,383 m)
4. Turn right on **NW Skyline Blvd** heading west (6,697 m)
5. Turn left on **NW Germantown Rd** to return north (1,320 m)
6. Turn right on **NW Bridge Avenue** to return to start (200 m)
