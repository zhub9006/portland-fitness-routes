# Leif Erikson Drive Freedom Trail Walk

**Route Type:** 🚶 Walking  
**Distance:** ~4.4 mi (7.1 km) out-and-back  
**Elevation Gain:** ~300 ft (90 m)  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Surface:** Paved footway

## Route Description

Walk the full length of Leif Erikson Drive in both directions for a satisfying out-and-back walk. This route covers the complete car-free loop road, giving you the full Forest Park ridgeline experience on foot. The car-free road is perfect for unhurried walking without worrying about traffic.

## Turn-by-Turn Directions

1. **Start** at Leif Erikson Dr & Skidmore Street (45.571, -122.772)
2. Walk south on Leif Erikson Drive — car-free road
3. Pass through the tree canopy tunnel
4. Continue past Lower Macleay Park
5. Follow the road as it curves around the park's eastern edge
6. Turn around at the western end of the road
7. Walk back to the starting point

## Key Highlights

- 🌳 **Canopy tunnel:** Lush, shaded walkway
- 👪 **No cars:** Safe for all ages and skill levels
- 🎨 **Fall colors: Spectacular autumn foliage
- 🏙️ **Panoramic views:** Forest and city from the ridgeline
