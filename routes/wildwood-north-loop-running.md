# Wildwood North Loop Run

**Route Type:** Running  
**Distance:** ~11,000 m (~6.8 mi)  
**Elevation Gain:** ~800 ft  
**Duration:** ~2.5 hrs  

## Overview

The ultimate Forest Park run! Combines the Wildwood Trail, Leif Erikson Drive, and Ridge loop into one continuous circuit. This is a challenging but rewarding route that covers the best of everything Forest Park has to offer — old-growth forest, ridge panoramas, and Creek crossings.

## Turn-by-Turn Directions

1. Start at **Lower Macleay Park** (45.534, -122.713)
2. Follow **Wildwood Trail** north to **Witch's Castle** (3,712 m)
3. Continue on Wildwood Trail to **Pittock Mansion** (4,923 m from start)
4. Descend via **Pittock Drive** to **NW Cornell Road**
5. Head south on **NW Cornell Road** to **NW Bridge Avenue** (1,184 m)
6. Follow **NW Bridge Avenue** to **NW Saint Helens Road** (945 m)
7. Follow **NW Saint Helens Road** south to **NW Nicolai Street** (3,263 m)
8. Connect to **NW Germantown Rd** heading west (1,320 m)
9. Continue on **NW Ridge Avenue** to complete the circuit (1,320 m)
10. Return to start via connector roads
