# Forest Park to Germantown Extended Walk 🥾

**Route Type:** Long-distance Walking / Hiking
**OSM Verified Distance:** 16,894 m (10.5 miles)
**Estimated Duration:** ~1,459 seconds (~24 min at faster pace)
**Elevation Gain:** Significant (~500-600 ft)
**Difficulty:** Moderate to Challenging
**Best For:** Trail runners, experienced hikers, long training rides

## Route Description

An extended walking route from Forest Park core eastward through Germantown Road, connecting to northern Portland neighborhoods. This serious long-distance route explores deeper forest trails and ridge roads, crossing the iconic Saint Johns Bridge and threading through the old-growth forest.

## Start / End Point

**Start:** Forest Park Core
- GPS: 45.575085, -122.759718

**End:** NW Germantown Road / Persimmon Place area
- GPS: 45.570, -122.769

## Seasonal Notes

- **Spring:** Muddy; trekking poles recommended
- **Summer:** Bring 1-2L water minimum; cool forest temps
- **Fall:** Spectacular Germantown Road foliage
- **Winter:** Limited daylight — plan shorter ride

## Safety Tips

- Long route — stay hydrated and fueled
- Cell service intermittent in deep forest
- Tell someone your route and expected return
- Saint Johns Bridge has dedicated pedestrian/bike lanes
- Consider shortening to Germantown area for a walk/bike
