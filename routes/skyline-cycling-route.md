# Forest Park Skyline Cycling Route ⭐

## Overview
- **Distance:** ~12.2 miles
- **Difficulty:** Moderate (skilled bike handling on narrow ridge roads)
- **Duration:** ~19 minutes at avg pace / ~21 min actual
- **Surface:** Paved roads (Skyline, Germantown roads)
- **Elevation Gain:** ~1,400 ft

## Route Description
The scenic spine of Forest Park cycling! Start on NW 53rd Drive, continue to NW Thompson Road, then hit **Northwest Skyline Boulevard** for 6 miles of beautiful ridge riding. Widening to Germantown Road before descending to West Burnside Road and returning via Southwest streets.

## Highlights
- Sweeping ridge-line views of Portland metro
- Washington Park and Mt. Hood visible on clear days
- Wildflower meadows in spring
- Car-free sections on Skyline Boulevard (weekday mornings best)
- Multiple panoramic viewpoints

## Best Practices
- **Best time:** Weekday mornings (less crowded)
- Avoid weekends when Skyline can be busy with pedestrians
- Skilled bike handling required — narrow ridge road sections
- Bring water — limited services along the route
- Visibility can be limited in forest sections at dawn/dusk

## Safety Reminders
- Bear right on shared roads
- Watch for pedestrians on Skyline Blvd (especially weekends)
- Cell coverage spotty in upper ridge areas
- Muddy conditions Nov–May — use wider tires
