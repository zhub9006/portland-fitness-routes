# Skyline Cycling Route

**Route Type:** Cycling | **Distance:** ~12.2 mi (19.6 km) | **Elevation Gain:** ~800 ft (244 m) | **Difficulty:** Moderate

## Overview

A scenic ridge ride along NW Skyline Boulevard offering panoramic views of the Portland skyline, Willamette Valley, and (on clear days) Mt. Hood. This route connects Forest Park's western boundary with the broader Portland bike network via Germantown Road.

## Key Terrain
- **Climbing:** NW Germantown Road is the big climb — steep but short (~1.5 mi)
- **Ridgeline:** Skyline Blvd offers unobstructed views of the Cascades
- **Surface:** Paved road with bike lanes on Skyline Boulevard
- **Traffic:** Moderate on Germantown Road; light on Laidlaw and Skyline

## Tips
- Best ridden early morning for clean air and fewer cars
- Check wind conditions — ridgeline can be breezy
- The Skyline Blvd section has good sight lines but blind corners near the ridge
- Bring a wind jacket; temperatures can drop 10–15°F on the exposed ridgeline
