# NW Saint Helens Road Walking Route

A historic walking route connecting Washington Park to Forest Park via NW Saint Helens Road.

## Route Overview

- **Type:** Walking
- **Total Distance:** 2,896 m (~1.8 mi) one-way
- **Elevation Gain:** ~200 ft
- **Difficulty:** Easy to Moderate
- **Surface:** Road with sidewalk; mixed residential and park environments

---

## Route Description

NW Saint Helens Road is a historic road that connects Washington Park to Forest Park. This route offers a lovely walk through a mix of residential neighborhoods and green spaces, culminating in the forested areas of Forest Park.

### Turn-by-Turn Directions

1. Start at Washington Park (45.5751, -122.7597)
2. Head west along NW Saint Helens Road (3,072 m)
   - Beautiful tree-lined street with sidewalks
   - Mix of residential homes and green spaces
3. Turn southwest onto NW Kittridge Avenue (370 m)
   - Short residential stretch
4. Continue onto the trail head area near Forest Park
   - The road transitions into more natural surroundings
5. Complete the route at the Forest Park Nature Center area

### Key Highlights
- 🌳 Historic tree-lined road with sidewalks
- 🏡 Mix of residential and park environments
- 🌲 Natural transition into Forest Park's forested areas
- 📸 Washington Park views at the start
- 🚶 Easy walk suitable for all skill levels

### Preparation Tips
- Wear comfortable walking shoes
- The road has sidewalks for most of the route
- Can be combined with a Forest Park loop for a longer walk
