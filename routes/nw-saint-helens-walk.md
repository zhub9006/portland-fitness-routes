**Highlights:**
- 🌲 Historic road connecting Washington Park to Forest Park
- 🏞️ Mix of residential neighborhoods and park connector trails
- 📸 Passes through Science Museum and Portland Art Museum areas
- 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Stroller-friendly wide sidewalk sections
- 🍂 Beautiful oak-lined avenue in autumn

**Running Club Tips:**
- Great out-and-back from the city center
- Add the Bridge Ave extension for a ~7.1 mi loop
- Walking club: meet at Washington Park and walk to the Field House
- Cross at NW 25th Ave — watch for traffic
