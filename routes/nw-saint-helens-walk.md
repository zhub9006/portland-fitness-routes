# NW Saint Helens Road Walking Route

**Route Type:** Walking  
**Distance:** ~3.3 mi one way (6.6 mi out-and-back)  
**Elevation Gain:** ~200 ft  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Surface:** Paved road (low traffic)  
**Best Season:** Year-round  

---

## Overview

NW Saint Helens Road connects Washington Park to Forest Park through a quiet residential neighborhood. This out-and-back walk follows the historic road, offering a gentle introduction to the park without the steep trail climbs.

## Route Details

### Route
- **Distance:** ~3.3 mi one way (6.6 mi out-and-back)
- **Duration:** ~50 min walking one way / ~1 hr 40 min out-and-back
- **Start:** Washington Park / NW 23rd Ave
- **End:** Forest Park / NW Cornell Rd

### Turn-by-Turn (One Way)
1. Start at Washington Park / NW NW 23rd Ave
2. Head north on **NW 23rd Avenue** (residential)
3. Transition to **NW Saint Helens Road**
4. Continue north through the **Forest Park neighborhood**
5. Arrive at **NW Cornell Road** — Forest Park boundary

### Key Landmarks
- **Washington Park:** Starting point with Zoo and Japanese Garden nearby
- **Forest Park neighborhood:** Quiet, tree-lined streets
- **NW Cornell Road:** The park boundary — your turnaround point
