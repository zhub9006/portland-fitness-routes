# Forest Park to Downtown Cycling Route 🚴

## Overview

A thrilling descent from Forest Park into downtown Portland, ending at the Willamette River waterfront. This route is perfect for a post-ride cooldown — mostly downhill on the way in, with a climb back up into the park.

- **OSM Verified:** 9,440 m (to downtown); 6,544 m (from downtown)
- **Distance:** 9,440 m downhill (to downtown) / 6,544 m uphill (from downtown)
- **Elevation:** ~500 ft descent to downtown
- **Difficulty:** Easy (hilly finish)
- **Surface:** Paved roads, multi-use paths

## Training Tips

- **Descents:** Practice braking techniques on the St. Helens Rd descent — it can be fast
- **Uphill return:** The climb back is a solid 6.5 km — great for leg strength
- **Intervals:** Use the fast descent segments for recovery intervals between hill repeats

---

## Safety Notes

- Watch for traffic on the urban streets near downtown
- Use hand signals at every intersection
- The descent on St. Helens Rd is fast — control your speed
- Wet leaves on the descent can be slippery — brake early
