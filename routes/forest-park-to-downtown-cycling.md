# Forest Park to Downtown Cycling Route

**Route Type:** Cycling (Descent Ride)  
**Distance:** ~14.6 miles (23.5 km)  
**Elevation Gain:** ~900 ft (descent)  
**Difficulty:** Moderate (descent-focused)  
**Surface:** Paved road  
**Best Season:** Spring through Fall  

---

## Overview

This route starts in Forest Park and descends through the city's western hills, finishing at the Willamette River waterfront in downtown Portland. It's one of the most exhilarating cycling routes in the city — a mostly downhill cruise from the forest to the river.

## Route Details

### Full Route
- **Distance:** ~14.6 mi
- **Duration:** ~50-60 min cycling
- **Start:** Forest Park center (45.536, -122.739)
- **End:** Downtown Portland / Riverplace

### Turn-by-Turn
1. **Forest Park → NW Cornell Road** (108 m)
2. **NW Cornell Road** (3,741 m) — Westbound past park entrance
3. **NW Lovejoy Street** (138 m) — Short connector
4. **NW 25th Avenue** (576 m) — North through West Hills
5. **NW Vaughn → NW Wardway → NW Nicolai** (973 m) — Transition area
6. **NW Saint Helens Road** (5,593 m) — Major ridge road
7. **NW Bridge Avenue** (854 m) — Descend toward St. Johns
8. **Saint Johns Bridge** (1,161 m) — Cross the Willamette
9. **NW Philadelphia Ave** (103 m) — Post-bridge connector
10. **N Willamette Blvd** (179 m) — Riverside
11. **N Baltimore Avenue** (313 m) — Continue east
12. **Downtown Portland** — Naito Parkway / Riverplace

### Key Highlights
- **Elevation drop:** ~900 ft descent from Forest Park to downtown
- **Forest descent:** Start through deep old-growth forest — immediate immersion
- **West Hills switchbacks:** Classic Portland cycling terrain
- **St. Johns Bridge:** 420 ft tower views — the highlight of the route
- **River finish:** cruise into the waterfront — reward yourself with a post-ride coffee

## Tips

- This is a DOWNHILL ride — pace yourself on the climbs back
- Check brake pads before the descent — you'll need them
- The most technical section is NW Cornell Road (narrow, some gravel)
- Restrooms at St. Johns Bridge toll plaza
- Combine with Leif Erikson Drive for a full day ride
