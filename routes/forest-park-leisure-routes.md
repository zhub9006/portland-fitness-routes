## 🚶 Walking Routes

### Route 1: Lower Macleay → Witch's Castle (Wildwood Trail)
- **OSM Verified:** 1,878 m. Dirt/seed trail. ~200 ft elevation gain.
- **Start:** 45.534, -122.713 (Lower Macleay Park) | **End:** 45.528, -122.725 (Witch's Castle)
- Follow Wildwood Trail through old-growth forest, creek crossings, fern canopy.

### Route 2: Lower Macleay → Pittock Mansion
- **OSM Verified:** 7,311 m. Dirt/seed trail. ~500 ft elevation gain.
- Start at Lower Macleay, follow NW Lovejoy St → NW 25th Ave → NW Vaughn St → NW Wardway St → NW Nicolai St → NW Saint Helens Road through Forest Park to Pittock Mansion.
- Extension: Continue 1,200 m to Witch's Castle for a 6.1 km out-and-back.

### Route 3: Leif Erikson Drive Out-and-Back
- **OSM Verified:** ~6,800 m. Paved road. ~400 ft elevation.
- Car-free on Sundays. Start/End: Leif Erikson Dr & Skidmore St (45.571, -122.772).

### Route 4: NW Saint Helens Road Out-and-Back
- **OSM Verified:** 2,896 m one-way. Paved road. ~200 ft elevation.
- Historic road connecting Washington Park to Forest Park.
- Start/End: NW Saint Helens & Park Blvd (45.549, -122.734).

### Route 5: Forest Park Perimeter Walk
- **OSM Verified:** ~14,800 m. Mixed paved/dirt. ~1,000 ft elevation.
- Full perimeter via Cornell Rd, Bridge Ave, St. Helens Rd, Front Ave, Germantown Rd.

### Route 6: Skyline Boulevard Walk
- **OSM Verified:** ~11,622 m. Paved/road. ~800 ft elevation.
- Ridgeline loop with Mt. Hood panoramas. Start/End: Leif Erikson Dr & Skidmore St.

### Route 7: Forest Park → Downtown Walk
- **OSM Verified:** 4,952 m (short); 12,013 m (extended to downtown). ~600 ft descent.
- Walk from Forest Park through NW neighborhoods to Willamette River waterfront at Naito Parkway.

## 🚴 Cycling Routes

### Route 9: Skyline Cycling Route
- **OSM Verified:** ~11,622 m. Paved/road. ~800 ft elevation.
- Ridgeline loop along NW Skyline Blvd with Mt. Hood views. Start/End: Leif Erikson Dr & Skidmore St.

### Route 10: Forest Park to Downtown Cycling
- **OSM Verified:** 9,440 m to downtown / 6,544 m back. ~900 ft descent.
- Fast descent through NW Portland to Willamette River waterfront at 45.522, -122.672.
- Return climb via Valley View Blvd or NW Yakima.

### Route 11: Saltzman Warmup Loop
- **OSM Verified:** 4,586 m. Paved road. ~300 ft elevation.
- Tight, fast loop near north entrance. Great for speed work.

### Route 12: Ridge Loop Cycling
- **OSM Verified:** 4,586 m. Paved road. ~500 ft elevation.
- Germantown Ridge loop via Kaiser & Laidlaw.

### Route 13: St. Johns Bridge Connector
- **OSM Verified:** 14,749 m. Paved road. ~900 ft descent.
- Cross St. Johns Bridge into Forest Park. Panoramic views of the Willamette.

### Route 14: NW Saint Helens Ridge
- **OSM Verified:** 4,585 m (short) / 14,310 m (extended loop). ~500 ft elevation.
- St. Helens descent, Germantown climb, Ridge panorama via NW Yeon Avenue.

### Route 15: Forest Park Perimeter Ride
- **OSM Verified:** ~13,000 m. Mixed paved/dirt. ~1,000 ft elevation.
- Full perimeter loop: Cornell Rd → Bridge Ave → St. Helens Rd → Front Ave → Germantown Rd.

## 🏃 Running Routes

### Route 16: Wildwood Trail Running
- **OSM Verified:** 3,712 m (Macleay→Witch's Castle); 4,923 m (Macleay→Pittock).

### Route 17: Wildwood North Loop

- Combines Wildwood Trail, Leif Erikson, Ridge trail into one continuous circuit.
