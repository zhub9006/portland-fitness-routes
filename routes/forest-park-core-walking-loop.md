# Forest Park Core Walking Loop

**Type:** Walking / Jogging / Running  
**Difficulty:** Easy to Moderate  
**Best Season:** Year-round

---

## Route: Forest Park Core Loop via NW Saint Helens Rd, NW 25th Ave, NW Cornell Rd

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Full Loop Distance** | 11,307 m (~7.0 mi) |
| **Walking Duration** | ~90 min walking |
| **OSM Verified** | ✅ Foot routing via NW Saint Helens Rd, NW 25th Ave, NW Cornell Rd |

**Coordinates:**
- Forest Park Center: 45.536, -122.739
- NW Bridge Ave & St. Helens: 45.557, -122.770
- NW Cornell & Lovejoy: 45.533, -122.740

**Route Segments:**
| Segment | Distance | Road |
|---------|----------|------|
| Forest Park Center → NW St. Helens Rd | ~2,500 m | NW Cornell Rd → NW St. Helens Rd |
| NW St. Helens Rd → NW Bridge Ave (north) | ~3,300 m | NW St. Helens Rd → NW Bridge Ave |
| NW Bridge Ave → NW 25th Ave | ~1,600 m | NW Bridge Ave → NW 25th Ave |
| NW 25th Ave → NW Cornell Rd (south) | ~3,907 m | NW 25th Ave → NW Lovejoy St → NW Cornell Rd |

**Highlights:**
- 🌲 Complete loop through the heart of Forest Park
- 🌳 Passes through old-growth forest sections on the western leg
- 🚶‍♂️ Out-and-back segments let you customize distance
- 🚫 Car-free on Sundays for the St. Helens/Bridge Ave sections

**Running Club Notes:**
- Great group walk — meet at Forest Park Field House and follow the loop
- Do half the loop for a ~35 min warm-up
- Add the Ridge Loop extension for an extra 4.6 km with hills
- Weekday mornings: combine with Wildwood Trail spurs for training runs
