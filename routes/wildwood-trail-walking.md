## Highlights
- Flat, accessible walking path along the south side of Forest Park
- Connects directly to the Lower Macleay Trailhead and Washington Park
- Crosses through residential neighborhoods with trees
- Takes ~60 min at walking pace (4 km/h)
- Groups of 15+ likely cannot take this exact route easily due to narrow residential streets; consider the Leif Erikson loop instead for large groups
