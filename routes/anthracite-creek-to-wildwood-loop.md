# Anthracite Creek to Wildwood Loop

**Type:** Running / Walking  
**Difficulty:** Moderate  
**Best Season:** Year-round (cooler in canopy)

---

## Route Overview

A connecting loop that links the Anthracite Creek Trail with the Wildwood Trail system, creating a comprehensive route through diverse Forest Park terrain. This route combines creek-side walking with ridge-line climbing and old-growth forest traverses.

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Total Distance** | ~8,500 m (~5.3 mi) |
| **Elevation Gain** | ~800 ft (rolling) |
| **Duration (Running)** | ~35-40 min |
| **Duration (Walking)** | ~80-90 min |
| **OSM Verified** | ✅ Foot routing via Anthracite Creek Trail, Upper Macleay, Wildwood Trail |

**Coordinates:**

- Upper Macleay Junction: 45.532, -122.718

## Route Segments

### Segment 1: Anthracite Creek → Upper Macleay (Low Ascent)
| Segment | Distance | Trail |
|---------|----------|-------|
| Anthracite Creek Trailhead → Macleay area | ~1,200 m | Anthracite Creek Trail / Lower Macleay Trail |
| Lower Macleay → Upper Macleay | ~800 m | Lower Macleay Trail |

**Highlights:**
- 🌿 Seasonal creek crossings along Anthracite Creek
- 🌲 Douglas fir and western redcedar canopy
- 🦋 Better wildlife viewing in the morning hours

### Segment 2: Upper Macleay → Wildwood Trail Junction (Steady Climb)
| Segment | Distance | Trail |
|---------|----------|-------|
| Upper Macleay → NW Lovejoy St | ~500 m | Upper Macleay Trail |
| NW Lovejoy St → Wildwood Trail junction | ~1,800 m | NW Lovejoy St → Wildwood Trail |

**Highlights:**
- ⛰️ Moderate climb from Macleay to Wildwood junction
- 🌳 Transition from forest to ridge views

### Segment 3: Wildwood Trail Circuit (Ridge + Descent)
| Segment | Distance | Trail |
|---------|----------|-------|
| Wildwood Junction → Witch's Castle | ~1,800 m | Wildwood Trail |
| Witch's Castle → Pittock Mansion | ~1,200 m | Wildwood Trail (continued) |
| Pittock → Wildwood rejoin | ~1,200 m | Wildwood Trail / Pittock Dr |

**Highlights:**
- 🏚️ Witch's Castle (Stone House) — iconic photo op
- 🏔️ Pittock Mansion — panoramic city views at the top
- 🌲 Old-growth forest with massive moss-covered trees

---

## Running Club Tips

- **Warm-up:** Start with a run along Anthracite Creek (flat, shaded) for 1-2 miles
- **Speed work:** Use the Wildwood Trail climb for hill repeats
- **Cool-down:** Pittock Mansion is a great turnaround point for timed efforts
- **Navigation:** Trail markers are well-placed; stay on Wildwood for the main ridge
- **Safety:** Carry water — there are no fountains on the upper trail sections
- **Seasonal:** Fall colors are spectacular on the Wildwood Trail portions
