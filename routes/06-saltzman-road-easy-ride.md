# Saltzman Road Warm-Up Ride — Cycling Route (Easy)

**Category:** Cycling  
**Distance:** ~2.1 miles (out and back / point-to-point)  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Surface:** Paved road + park road  
**Duration:** ~3–4 minutes at cycling pace; ~10–15 min with warm-up stops  
**Best For:** Quick warm-up/lap/recovery before or after main rides

---

## Route Overview

This short connector ride threads from **Forest Park's northern edge** southward along the signature **Northwest Saint Helens Road** and terminates near **Northwest Saltzman Road** — a quiet residential collector that serves as the perfect warm-up loop. It's the go-to route for Portland cyclists who want a quick spin before tackling the bigger Forest Park routes (Skyline spin or Leif Erikson).

The route includes a brief section on the famous **car-free Leif Erikson Drive** tree tunnel (when vehicles are stopped!) and a scenic 1.6 mi asphalt arterial through old-growth forest.

---

## Directions

1. Start from any Forest Park northern parking/pullout area (near **Washington School** or the **Forest Park SIGN**)
2. Head **south** on the access road to join **Northwest Saint Helens Road**
3. Ride south on **Northwest Saint Helens Road** (1.6 mi) — gentle descent
4. Pass through the southern **Leif Erikson tree tunnel** (verify car-free status!)
5. Continue south toward the **Northwest Saltzman Road** junction
6. End point: Northwest Saltzman Road (45.561, -122.750)  
   - Quiet, low-traffic residential street perfect for easy spinouts
   - Parking available along Saltzman for extended stop/swim/depot

**Round-trip from nearest parking:** ~2.1 mi  
**Duration:** 3–15 minutes depending on warm-up/stop count

## When to Ride

- 🚗 **Car-free recommended:** Monday, Tuesday, Thursday (all day) — road signs posted
- 🏃 **Not recommended** when vehicles are in heavy flow except如果你 stay single-file right
- 🌅 **Best time:** Early morning (6–8 AM) for multiple clear user-groups sharing
- 🚲 **E-bikes:** Permitted when the road is open to cars with pedal-assist only

## Post-Ride Extension

From Saltzman Road (45.561, -122.750), you can:
- 🚗 **Drive** over to the western portion of Skyline Blvd for the full Skyline Spin loop
- 🤸 **Shuttle** back north for multiple complete laps
- ☕ **Head west** to Saltzman residential streets + cafes (pedestrian scale)
- 🏃 **Connect** to Lower Macleay via Summerville/Saint Helens for a longer walk
