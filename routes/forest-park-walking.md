# Forest Park Walking Routes

Detailed walking routes with OSM coordinates for the Portland Running Club.

## Route 1: Basin Road to Pittock Mansion (Easy)
- **Distance:** ~6 miles out-and-back
- **Elevation Gain:** ~1,500 ft
- **Start:** Bethel Rd & NW 29th Ave (45.525, -122.720)
- **End:** NW Pittock Mansion (45.620, -122.753)
- **Terrain:** Paved path, dirt trail

Classic Portland out-and-back through lush forest to the historic Pittock Mansion with stunning views of the city and Mt. Hood.

## Route 2: Wildwood Trail Loop (Moderate)
- **Distance:** ~5.1 miles (loop)
- **Elevation Gain:** ~800 ft
- **Start:** Shadowfjord Trailhead (45.571, -122.772)
- **Terrain:** Dirt trail, rocky sections

Portland's premier trail walking route through old-growth forest. Can be muddy after rain — bring your own water.
