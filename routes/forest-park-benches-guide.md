## Tips for Walking Groups

- Space out group walks with 10-15 min rest every 1.5 miles
- The Forest Park Field House (45.583, -122.778) is the best starting/ending point
- Pittock Mansion restrooms are open after 10 AM daily
- Lower Macleay has a water fountain near the trailhead
