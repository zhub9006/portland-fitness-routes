## 🚶 Walking Routes

### Route 1: Lower Macleay Park → Witch's Castle (Wildwood Trail)
- **Verified Distance:** 3,379 m (2.1 mi)
- **Duration:** ~45–55 min walk
- **Elevation Gain:** ~150 ft
- **Surface:** Dirt/seed trail
- **OSM Verified:** Via NW Lovejoy St → Lower Macleay Trail → Wildwood Trail
- **Start:** 45.534, -122.713 (Lower Macleay Park)
- **End:** 45.528, -122.725 (Witch's Castle / Stone House ruins)
- **Highlights:** Old-growth forest, fern-covered trails, moss-covered logs, creek crossings, historic Stone House ruins
- **Trail Etiquette:** Hikers have right-of-way on narrow paths; ring bell or announce when passing others
