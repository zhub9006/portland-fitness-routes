## Methodology

All routes were verified using the OpenStreetMap (OSM) routing engine:
1. Headquarters coordinates from OSM geocoding (Forest Park center: 45.571, -122.772)
2. Turn-by-turn directions computed via OSM pathfinder
3. Distances calculated from route geometry
4. Elevation gain estimated from route profile
5. Duration estimates based on standard walking (5 km/h) and cycling (15 km/h) speeds
