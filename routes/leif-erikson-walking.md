# Leif Erikson Drive Walking Route

**Type:** Walking / Jogging / Rolling  
**Difficulty:** Easy  
**Best Season:** Year-round (car-free Sundays are best)

## Route: Bridge Avenue → Saint Helens Road Out-and-Back

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **One-Way Distance** | 4,390 m (~2.7 mi) |
| **Walking Duration** | ~5.7 min (one way) |
| **Full Loop Distance** | ~10,400 m (~6.5 mi) |
| **OSM Verified** | ✅ Foot routing via NW Bridge Ave, NW St. Helens Rd |

**Coordinates:**
- Bridge Ave Start: 45.5769, -122.7614
- St. Helens Rd Midpoint: 45.549, -122.734

**Route Segments:**
| Segment | Distance | Road |
|---------|----------|------|

**Highlights:**
- 🚗 **Car-free** — Sundays are closed to vehicles (seasonally)
- 🌳 Tree canopy tunnel in summer — stunning shade
- 🍂 Spectacular fall colors along the entire route
- 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family-friendly wide path — stroller and wheelchair accessible
- 📸 Mt. Hood views on clear days from the eastern ridge section

**Running Club Notes:**
- Perfect warm-up loop — flat, predictable terrain
- Out-and-back format lets you control distance
- On Sundays, you can do the full ~10.4 km loop without car concerns
- Add a Wildwood Trail spur for elevation work
