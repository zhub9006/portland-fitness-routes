## Route Description

Leif Erikson Drive is a car-free loop road that wraps around the upper portion of Forest Park. It's the most popular walking/jogging route in the park — shaded by a dense canopy tunnel in summer and blazing with fall color each autumn.

### Direction: Clockwise (Recommended)

1. **Start:** NW Saint Helens Rd & Leif Erikson Dr (45.549, -122.734) — north loop entrance
2. Head north on Leif Erikson Drive through a dramatic **canopy tunnel** in summer
3. Follow the road as it curves west and then south along the ridgeline
4. Pass through open meadows with panoramic city views
5. Continue south to NW Skidmore St at the top of the drive
6. Connect via Germantown Rd and St. Helens Rd back to the starting point

### Direction: Counter-clockwise

1. Start at NW Skidmore St & Leif Erikson Dr
2. Head north along the ridgeline
3. Complete the loop back to the starting point

## Key Landmarks

- **Canopy Tunnel** — densely tree-shaded section (~45.555, -122.742); magical in summer
- **NW Skidmore St** — turnaround point at the north end (45.571, -122.772)
- **Wildwood Trail intersection** — connects to forest interior trails
- **Forest Park Field House** — community center near the east end (45.583, -122.778)
- **Germantown Rd descent** — steep hill with rewarding city views
