# Leif Erikson Drive Walking Route 🚶

**Route Type:** Walking/Jogging | **Difficulty:** Easy | **Elevation:** Gentle grade

---

## Route Overview

Leif Erikson Drive is a **car-free loop road** through Forest Park, offering one of the best urban walking/jogging experiences in Portland. The road is closed to vehicles on Sundays, making it perfect for family-friendly walks and group runs.

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Distance (One Way) | 4,390 m (~2.7 mi) |
| Distance (Full Loop) | ~10,400 m (~6.5 mi) |
| Duration (One Way) | ~5.7 min at walking pace; ~5 min at jogging pace |
| Duration (Full Loop) | ~57 min walk; ~25 min jog |
| Mode | Foot |
| Elevation | Gently undulating, ~150-200 ft total |

## Detailed Segments

### Segment 1: Lower Macleay Park → NW Skyline Blvd
- **Distance:** 3,763 m
- **Road:** North West Royal Boulevard → North West Skyline Boulevard
- **Surface:** Paved, well-maintained
- **Notes:** Follows the western ridge with forest views on both sides

### Segment 2: NW Skyline Blvd → NW Thompson Road
- **Distance:** 690 m
- **Road:** North West Thompson Road
- **Notes:** Descent toward the northern entrance area

### Segment 3: NW Thompson Road → Wildwood Trailhead (Return)
- **Distance:** 690 m
- **Road:** North West Thompson Road → North West Greenleaf Road
- **Notes:** Connects back to the southern trailhead area

## How to Use This Route

### 1. Out-and-Back (Easy 30-min Walk)
1. Start at **Lower Macleay Park** (45.528, -122.746)
2. Walk west on **North West Royal Boulevard** (342 m)
3. Follow the road as it becomes **North West Skyline Boulevard** (3,763 m)
4. Turn around at any point and walk back
5. **Recommended turnaround:** NW Thompson Road intersection (~2.0 mi one way)

### 2. Full Loop (65-min Walk or 25-min Jog)
1. Start at **Lower Macleay Park**
2. Follow Leif Erikson Drive west on Royal Boulevard → Skyline Boulevard
3. Continue to NW Thompson Road
4. Loop back via Greenleaf Road and Spring Avenue
5. Return to Lower Macleay Park

### 3. Family Walk (Stroller-Friendly)
- The entire loop is paved and stroller-friendly
- Start at the **Forest Park Field House** entrance
- Walk clockwise for easier hill ascents
- Stop at the **Natural Areas parking lot** for a rest

## Seasonal Notes

| Season | Tips |
|--------|------|
| 🌸 Spring (Mar-May) | Wildflower blooms along the road; mild temps; most popular |
| ☀️ Summer (Jun-Aug) | Dense canopy creates a cool tunnel effect; bring water |
| 🍂 Fall (Sep-Nov) | Spectacular foliage; less crowds; perfect for photography |
| ❄️ Winter (Dec-Feb) | Icy patches possible; wear reflective gear; fewer users |

## Group Walk Tips
- 👥 Walk single file on narrow sections
- 🔔 Ring bell or announce when passing (trail etiquette)
- 📱 Keep phones away - enjoy the scenery!
- ☕ Post-walk coffee at **World Cup Coffee** on NW 23rd Ave (5 min drive from Lower Macleay)
