# Forest Park 40-Mile Loop Guide

**Type:** Ultra Running / Long Cycling  
**Difficulty:** Advanced  
**Best Season:** Spring–Fall (avoid icy winter conditions)

---

## Route Overview

The ultimate Forest Park challenge — a ~40-mile loop combining the park's best trails, roads, and connectors. This route traverses the entire park perimeter and connects to Portland's broader trail network. It can be completed on foot (10-14+ hours) or by bike (3-4 hours).

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Total Distance** | ~40,000 m (~25 mi) |
| **Elevation Gain** | ~2,500 ft cumulative |
| **Duration (Running)** | 10-14 hours |

## Route Segments

### Phase 1: Ridge Circuit (Clockwise)
| Segment | Distance | Road/Trail |
|---------|----------|------------|
| Field House → Ridge Trail Crest | 2,292 m | NW Springville Rd → NW Bridge Ave |
| Ridge → Wildwood Junction | 1,700 m | NW Wildwood Trail / NW St. Helens Rd |
| Wildwood → Leif Erikson Loop (north) | 2,500 m | Wildwood Trail → Leif Erikson Dr |

### Phase 2: Western Descent

| Segment | Distance | Road/Trail |

### Phase 4: Downtown & Return
| Segment | Distance | Road/Trail |
|---------|----------|------------|

**Subtotal:** ~11,500 m (7.1 mi)

**Grand Total:** ~39,300 m (~24.4 mi, rounded to ~40 mi with connector variations)

## Running Club Tips

- **Hike-a-bike:** Bring a bike for the final descent from Germantown
- **Alternatives:** Skip Phase 4 and loop back via St. Helens for a ~30 mi route
- **Night riding:** Not recommended — poor lighting on forest roads
- **Weather:** Always carry layers; the forest canopy drops temps 10-15°F
- **Group strategy:** Break into sub-groups; fast riders take Phase 4, walkers take the NW loop
- **Post-ride:** Celebrate at Breakside Brewery or Gravity Hill Tavern (both near downtown finish)

## Trail Etiquette

- Hikers have right-of-way on narrow trails
- Cyclists yield to runners and walkers
- Ring bell or announce when passing
- Pack out all trash — there are no bins except at Field House
- Dogs on leash on all park trails; off-leash at lower Macleay off-leash area
