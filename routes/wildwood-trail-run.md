# Wildwood Trail Run

## Route Overview
- **Distance:** ~5.1 miles (loop)
- **Elevation Gain:** ~800 ft
- **Terrain:** Dirt trail, rocky sections
- **Difficulty:** 🟡 Moderate
- **Start:** Lower Macleay Trailhead or Shadowfjord Trailhead
- **GPS Coordinates:** 45.5709, -122.7716

## Description
The Wildwood Trail is Portland's premier trail running route, winding through old-growth forest. This loop combines the Wildwood Trail with the Lower Macleay Trail for a scenic forest experience. Perfect for cross-training and scenic long runs.

## Turn-by-Turn Directions
1. Start at Lower Macleay Trailhead (NW 29th Ave & NW Upshur St)
2. Take the Lower Macleay Trail north into the forest
3. Connect to the Wildwood Trail at the junction (~0.5 mi)
4. Follow the Wildwood Trail northwest through old-growth forest
5. Continue on the Wildwood Trail for ~2.5 miles
6. Exit onto NW Skyline Blvd and turn right (south)
7. Follow Skyline Blvd for ~0.5 miles
8. Turn left onto Newberry Road
9. Follow Newberry Road back to Lower Macleay area
10. Complete the loop back at the trailhead

## Best For
- Trail running
- Cross-training
- Scenic long runs

## Notes
- Can be muddy after rain — trail running shoes recommended
- No water fountains on trail — bring your own
- Watch for hikers on the trail — yield to pedestrians
- Sunday mornings are best for quiet running
