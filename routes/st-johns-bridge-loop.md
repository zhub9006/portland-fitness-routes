# St. Johns Bridge to Cathedral Park Loop

## Route Overview
- **Distance:** ~3.5 miles
- **Elevation Gain:** ~50 ft
- **Terrain:** Paved roads with bike lanes
- **Difficulty:** 🟢 Easy
- **Start:** St. Johns Bridge NW Service Road parking area
- **GPS Coordinates:** 45.595, -122.73

## Description
A scenic ride over the historic St. Johns Bridge, through Cathedral Park, and back along the waterfront. Great views of the Willamette River and the Portland skyline.

## Turn-by-Turn Directions
1. Start at St. Johns Bridge NW Service Road parking area
2. Cross the St. Johns Bridge (historic 1931 steel arch bridge)
3. Enjoy panoramic views of the Willamette River and Portland skyline
4. Continue on NW Bridge Avenue into Cathedral Park
5. Ride through Cathedral Park along the river
6. Exit the park onto NW St. Helens Road
7. Turn left (south) on NW St. Helens Road
8. Continue to the NW Service Road
9. Retrace your path back across the St. Johns Bridge to the start

## Best For
- Warm-up rides
- Scenic waterfront loops
- Club group rides

## Notes
- Use caution on the bridge — there is a narrow pedestrian/bike lane
- Great photo spots at the bridge approaches and in Cathedral Park
- Popular with runners and walkers on weekends
- The route is mostly flat — ideal for easy recovery rides
