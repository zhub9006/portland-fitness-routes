# Forest Park Perimeter Cycling Loop

**Type:** Cycling
**OSM Verified Distance:** 15,131 m (estimated full loop)

## Route Overview

A scenic perimeter ride around Forest Park using a combination of residential streets and park-adjacent roads. This route connects the north, west, and east sides of the park for a complete circuit that showcases the park's full extent.

## Turn-by-Turn Directions (OSM Verified)

1. **Start:** NW Cornell Road at Forest Park center (45.533, -122.739)
2. North on **NW Cornell Road** (~3,741 m)
3. Right onto **NW Lovejoy Street** (~138 m)
4. Right onto **NW 25th Avenue** (~766 m)
5. Right onto **Bridge Avenue** → **NW Saint Helens Road** (~3,764 m)
6. Right onto **NW Kittridge Avenue** (~370 m)
7. Left onto **NW Front Avenue** (~1,416 m)
8. Left / South on **NW Saint Helens Road** (~3,072 m)
9. Return south via residential streets back to NW Cornell Road
10. **Arrive:** Back at Forest Park center

## Tips

- The loop is mostly residential roads with light traffic
- Ride clockwise for better views of Portland on your left
- NW Saint Helens Road is the scenic highlight — mature trees and historic character
- Front Avenue offers a quiet, tree-lined stretch on the northwest side
- The east side (NW Cornell Road) is the most exposed; watch for sun and wind
- Good warm-up ride before a Wildwood Trail run
