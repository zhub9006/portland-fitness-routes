# Wildwood Ridge Walking Route

## Overview

A moderate-to-strenuous ridge walk from Forest Park center to the Wildwood Trail area through Portland's West Hills.

## Route Details

- **Distance:** 3.3 miles (5.3 km)
- **Difficulty:** Moderate-to-Strenuous (steady uphill + ridge exposure)
- **Duration:** ~64 minutes
- **Type:** Point-to-point walk
- **Elevation Gain:** ~800 ft

## Route Description

Starting from **Forest Park center** (45.571, -122.772), head west toward the ridge:

1. **Forest Park center → Northwest Saint Helens Road** (1.7 mi) – Follow the main park access road northward.
2. **Northwest Saint Helens Road → Northwest Bridge Avenue** (0.4 mi) – Turn east toward the bridge.
3. **Northwest Bridge Avenue → Northwest Germantown Road** (3.4 mi) – Continue east along the ridge.
4. **Northwest Germantown Road → Northwest Skyline Boulevard** (2.5 mi) – Ride the scenic spine.
5. **Northwest Skyline Boulevard → Northwest College Drive** (0.5 mi) – Arrive at the Wildwood Trail area.

## Safety Notes

- Ridge exposure – wind can be strong
- Carry water – limited refill points
- Best done in morning for optimal visibility
- Check trail conditions before heading out
