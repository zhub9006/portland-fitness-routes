# Forest Park Willamette River Circuit

**Type:** Walking / Cycling  
**Difficulty:** Easy to Moderate  
**Best Season:** Spring–Fall (avoid winter mud)

---

## Route Overview

A scenic multi-use loop connecting Forest Park's western trails with the Willamette River waterfront via the Springwater Corridor and NW Nicolai Street. Perfect for a post-run cool-down ride or a standalone trail walk.

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Total Distance** | ~14,500 m (~9.0 mi) |
| **Elevation Gain** | ~300 ft (gentle) |
| **Duration (Walking)** | ~150 min |
| **Duration (Cycling)** | ~40 min |
| **OSM Verified** | ✅ Combined foot+bike routing via NW Nicolai, Burnside, Springwater Corridor |

## Highlights

- 🌊 Willamette River waterfront views at Tom McCall Park
- 🌲 Springwater Corridor — paved multi-use path through the city
- 🌳 Forest Park western trails — shaded old-growth canopy
- 🦆 Portland's urban wildlife — herons, ducks, and raccoons along the river
- 🍺 End at Breakside Brewery (Portland's best brewery, right at the river)

---

## Running Club Tips

- **Walk version:** 2.5 hours at a leisurely pace — great for social walks after a run
- **Bike version:** 40 minutes of flat, fast riding — perfect warm-down
- **Combine with Route #3 (Forest Park to Downtown)** for a 16 mi ride
- **Watch for:** Construction on the Springwater Corridor — check for detours
- **Best time:** Late afternoon when the river glows gold with sunset
