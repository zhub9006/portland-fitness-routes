# Wildwood Trail: Macleay to Pittock Mansion 🌿🏛️

A rewarding walk from the Lower Macleay trailhead through Forest Park to the iconic Pittock Mansion, offering an extended wildflower-lined trail with panoramic summit views.

## Highlights

- 🌸 **Wildflower meadows** — seasonal blooms along the trail from March through June
- 🌲 **Forest canopy** — mixed old-growth and second-growth forest
- 🏛️ **Pittock Mansion** — 1914 French Renaissance estate with panoramic Willamette Valley views
- 🌊 **Creek crossings** — several small seasonal creeks to navigate
- 📸 **Photography spots** — excellent views from the mansion terrace

## Pittock Mansion Info

- **Hours:** Open seasonally (check portland.gov for current hours)
- **Admission:** Small fee
- **Parking:** Limited parking at mansion; street parking available on NW Pittock Dr
- **Amenities:** Restrooms, gift shop, café nearby

---

## Tips

- Start early in the morning to avoid midday heat on exposed sections
- The trail can be muddy after rain — waterproof footwear recommended
- Pittock Mansion has a small admission fee — budget $5–8
- Combine with a visit to the Pittock Mansion grounds for a full half-day outing
