# Wildwood Trail Run

**Route Type:** Running  
**Distance:** 3,712 m (2.3 mi) — Macleay to Witch's Castle  
**Elevation Gain:** ~500 ft  
**Duration:** ~10 min run / ~55 min walk  

## Overview

A moderate run on the Wildwood Trail from Lower Macleay Park to the Witch's Castle. This section of the trail offers a good mix of flat forest paths and some gentle climbs through old-growth trees. Perfect for a training run or recovery jog.

## Turn-by-Turn Directions

1. Start at **Lower Macleay Park** (45.534, -122.713) — trailhead at NW Cornell Road
2. Follow **Wildwood Trail** north (3,712 m)
3. Pass creek crossings at approximately 1.2 km and 2.5 km
4. Finish at **Witch's Castle** (45.528, -122.725)
