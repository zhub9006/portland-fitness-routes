# Wildwood Trail Running Route

**Type:** Running | **Category:** Trail Run

## Route 1: Lower Macleay → Witch's Castle
- **Verified Distance:** 3,712 m (~2.3 mi)
- **Duration:** ~35-40 min run
- **Elevation Gain:** ~500 ft
- **Surface:** Dirt/seed trail
- **OSM Verified:** Via NW Lovejoy St, Lower Macleay Trail, Wildwood Trail

### Turn-by-Turn
1. Start at Lower Macleay Park (45.534, -122.713)
2. Follow NW Lovejoy St into the park
3. Turn onto Lower Macleay Trail
4. Continue on Wildwood Trail
5. Arrive at Witch's Castle (Stone House) ruins (45.528, -122.725)

### Highlights
- Old-growth forest and creek crossings
- Moderate climb through the trees
- Stone House ruins make a great turnaround point

## Route 2: Lower Macleay → Pittock Mansion
- **Verified Distance:** 4,923 m (~3.1 mi)
- **Duration:** ~45-50 min run
- **Elevation Gain:** ~700 ft
- **Surface:** Dirt/seed trail
- **OSM Verified:** Via NW 25th Ave, Westover Rd, Pittock Dr, NW Pittock Avenue

### Turn-by-Turn
1. Start at Lower Macleay Park (45.534, -122.713)
2. Follow NW 25th Avenue into Washington Park
3. Take Westover Road
4. Cross Burnside Street
5. Follow NW Pittock Avenue
6. Continue to NW Pittock Drive
7. Arrive at Pittock Mansion (45.525, -122.716)

### Highlights
- Extended creek crossings
- Pittock Mansion at the summit
- More elevation gain for a challenging run
