# Wildwood Trail Running Route

**Route Type:** Running / **Distance:** 3,712 m (Macleay to Witch's Castle) or 4,923 m (Macleay to Pittock) / **Elevation:** ~500 ft (Route 1); ~700 ft (Route 2) / **Format:** Out-and-back

## Turn-by-Turn Directions

**Route 1 — Macleay to Witch's Castle:**
1. Start at **Lower Macleay Park** (45.534, -122.713)
2. Head west on **NW Cornell Road** (1,173 m)
3. Turn onto **Wildwood Trail** (108 m)
4. Follow Wildwood Trail through old-growth forest (3,498 m)
5. Arrive at **Witch's Castle** (45.528, -122.725)

**Route 2 — Macleay to Pittock Mansion:**
6. Continue on Wildwood Trail past Castle (1,211 m)
7. Turn onto **NW Skyline Boulevard** (2,720 m)
8. Continue via Burnside, Waterside Aves to Pittock Mansion (4,923 m total)
