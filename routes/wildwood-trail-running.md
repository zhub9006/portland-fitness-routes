# Wildwood Trail Running Loop

**Route Type:** Running | **Distance:** ~6.0 mi (9.7 km) | **Elevation Gain:** ~550 ft (170 m) | **Difficulty:** Moderate

## Overview

The iconic Wildwood Trail loop takes you through the heart of Forest Park, winding through old-growth Douglas fir forest with seasonal creek crossings and lush sword fern understory. This is the signature route for our running club.

## Route Details

| Segment | Distance | Notes |
|---------|----------|-------|
| Start: Wildwood Trail Main Entrance (NW Germantown Rd) | 0.0 mi | Trailhead parking available |
| Wildwood Trail (northbound) | ~2.0 mi | Old-growth forest, fern canopy |
| Upshur St connector | ~0.5 mi | Brief road section |
| Northwest Germantown Road | ~1.5 mi | Ridge section with city views |
| Wildwood Trail (southbound) | ~2.0 mi | Return through deep forest |

## Key Terrain
- **Creek crossings:** 3 seasonal crossings (may be muddy after rain)
- **Elevation:*** Gradual climbs with one steep section near Northwest Germantown Road
- **Surface:** Packed dirt trail, some rocky sections

## Tips
- Allow 45–60 minutes for the full loop at a moderate pace
- Bring water — there are no refill points on the trail
- Check for trail closures after heavy storms (downed trees are common)
- Yield to hikers and trail runners going the opposite direction
