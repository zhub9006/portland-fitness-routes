# Leif Erikson Walking Loop

## Overview

A peaceful, car-free walking loop along Leif Erikson Drive through the old-growth heart of Forest Park.

## Route Details

- **Distance:** 4.2 miles (6.8 km) loop
- **Difficulty:** Easy (car-free, flat path)
- **Duration:** ~55 minutes
- **Type:** Loop
- **Surface:** Paved

## Route Description

Starting at the **Leif Erikson & Saint Helens junction** (45.549, -122.734):

1. **Trailhead parking → Northwest Saint Helens Road** (0.3 mi) – Access the path via the main parking area.
2. **Northwest Saint Helens Road → Northwest Leif Erikson Drive** (0.2 mi) – Turn onto the car-free path.
3. **Leif Erikson Drive loop** (3.7 mi) – Complete the scenic loop through old-growth forest.
4. Return via same path to trailhead.

## Highlights

- 🌲 Tunnels of old-growth Douglas fir and Western red cedar
- 🏞️ Creekside riding/walking along Balch Creek
- 🦌 Wildlife spotting opportunities (deer, coyotes)
- 🍃 Fern canopy overhead throughout the route

## Car-Free Hours

**Leif Erikson Drive is closed to vehicles:**
- Monday, Tuesday, Thursday: All day
- Saturday: After noon
- **Always verify closure status before cycling or skating**

## Safety Notes

- ✅ Car-free path – safe for walkers and families
- ⚠️ Leash required for dogs on all Portland park trails
- 🚸 Watch for shared use – hikers and cyclists present
- 🌧️ Trail can be muddy Nov–May; wear appropriate footwear
