# Forest Park to Downtown Portland Walking Route 🏙️🚶

An extended walking route from Forest Park through NW Portland neighborhoods down to the Willamette River waterfront and downtown core.

**OSM Verified:** 12,013 m (~7.5 mi) | **Duration:** ~17 min walk (OSM foot mode)

## Route Segments

### Segment 1: Forest Park to NW 23rd Avenue
- **Distance:** ~4,952 m
- **Route:** Head east from Forest Park through residential NW neighborhoods
- **Surface:** Sidewalks, paved roads

### Segment 2: NW 23rd Avenue to Burnside Street
- **Distance:** ~3,000 m
- **Route:** Continue south on NW 23rd Ave to Burnside, then east to downtown
- **Surface:** Paved, downtown sidewalks

### Segment 3: Burnside to Willamette River Waterfront
- **Distance:** ~4,061 m
- **Route:** Follow Burnside St east, then transition to waterfront paths and Naito Pkwy
- **Surface:** Paved, multi-use Path Along the Willamette

---

## Turn-by-Turn Directions

1. Start at **Forest Park center** (near NW Cornell Rd & NW 33rd Ave)
2. Head east on **NW Cornell Rd** — briefly (approach from west side of the park)
3. Turn right (south) onto **NW 23rd Avenue** (1,200 m)
4. Continue on **NW 23rd Ave** through residential Keister neighborhood (1,500 m)
5. Turn left onto **NW Lovejoy St** heading east (800 m)
6. Turn right onto **NW 20th Ave** heading south (500 m)
7. Turn left onto **NW Kearney St** heading east (1,000 m)
8. Continue onto **NW Johnson St** heading east (1,200 m)
9. Turn right onto **US-26/Burnside Street** heading east (2,000 m)
10. Take **Burnside** east over the Steel Bridge toward downtown
11. Exit onto **Naito Parkway** heading south along the waterfront (1,500 m)
12. Continue along **Tom McCall Waterfront Park** paths to the Willamette River crossing
13. Arrive at **Willamette River Waterfront** — enjoy the views!
