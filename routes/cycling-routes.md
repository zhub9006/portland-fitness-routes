# Cycling Routes Around Forest Park, Portland

## 1. Forest Park Skyline Cycling Route
- **Distance:** ~12.2 miles
- **Difficulty:** Moderate (skilled bike handling on narrow ridge roads)
- **Start/End:** 45.575, -122.760 (Forest Park center area)
- **Route:** NW 53rd Drive → NW Thompson Road → NW Skyline Boulevard (scenic spine!) → NW Germantown Road → West Burnside Road → SW Barnes Road → SW Taylor Street
- **Highlights:** Ridge views, multiple panoramic viewpoints, car-free Skyline sections
- **Best:** Weekday mornings

## 2. Leif Erikson Drive Cycling Loop
- **Distance:** ~4.2 miles
- **Difficulty:** Easy (car-free path, families)
- **Start:** 45.549, -122.734 (Leif Erikson & Saint Helens junction)
- **Route:** NW Saint Helens Road car-free loop through old-growth forest
- **Highlights:** Old-growth tree tunnels, creekside riding, fern canopy
- **Note:** Closed to vehicles Mon/Tue/Thu all day, Sat after noon

## 3. Forest Park to Willamette Valley Route
- **Distance:** ~14.6 miles
- **Difficulty:** Moderate (dramatic downhill, return is uphill)
- **Start:** 45.575, -122.760 → End: Downtown Portland (~45.520, -122.680)
- **Route:** NW Saint Helens Road → West Burnside Road → city to Willamette River

## 4. Saltzman Road Warm-Up Loop
- **Distance:** ~2.1 miles
- **Difficulty:** Easy
- **Start/End:** 45.575, -122.760
- **Route:** NW Saint Helens Road (1.6 mi) → NW Saltzman Road (0.5 mi)

## 5. Cathedral Park Loop
- **Distance:** ~7 miles round trip
- **Difficulty:** Moderate
- **Start:** 45.580, -122.762 → End: 45.591, -122.752 (Cathedral Park)
- **Route:** Via Saint Johns Bridge with panoramic river views

## Safety
- Leash required for dogs
- Bear right on paths
- Car-free hours: Leif Erikson Drive closed to vehicles Mon/Tue/Thu, Sat after noon
- Spotty cell coverage deep in forest
