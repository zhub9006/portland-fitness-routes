## 1. Forest Park Fire Roads Circuit (Moderate) 🟡

| Detail | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Distance** | ~15 miles |
| **Elevation Gain** | ~800 ft |
| **Terrain** | Gravel fire roads, some single-track |
| **Est. Time** | 2–3 hours |

- **Start:** Lower Macleay parking area
- **Route:** Circuit through the heart of Forest Park using the fire road network. Combines Leif Erikson Drive with fire roads like Newberry Road and Lower Macleay Road.
- **Key Roads:** Leif Erikson Drive, Newberry Road, Lower Macleay Road
- **Best For:** Mountain biking, gravel cycling, trail endurance

## 2. Leif Erikson Drive Full Length (Easy-Moderate) 🟢

| Detail | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Distance** | ~11 miles (one way) |
| **Elevation Gain** | ~1,100 ft (northward) |
| **Terrain** | Paved, car-free |
| **Est. Time** | 45–60 minutes |

- **Start:** Burnside St / NW 26th Ave
- **End:** Gorum Ridge area
- **Best For:** Road cycling, long rides, family rides
- **⚠️ Notes:** Car-free Weekdays (dawn to ~10am) and all day Sundays. Popular with runners too — share the road!

## 4. St. Johns Bridge to Cathedral Park Loop (Easy) 🟢

| Detail | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Distance** | ~3.5 miles |
| **Elevation Gain** | ~50 ft |
| **Terrain** | Paved roads with bike lanes |
| **Est. Time** | 20–25 minutes |

- **Start:** St. Johns Bridge NW Service Road
- **Best For:** Warm-up rides, scenic waterfront loops, club group rides
- **⚠️ Notes:** Use caution on the bridge — there is a narrow pedestrian/bike lane. Great views of the Willamette River and Portland skyline.

---

## 5. NW Skyline Blvd Loop (Advanced) 🔴

| Detail | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Distance** | ~8 miles |
| **Elevation Gain** | ~1,500 ft |
| **Terrain** | Paved road, some steep sections |
| **Est. Time** | 45–60 minutes |

- **Start:** NW Germantown Road & NW Skyline Blvd
- **Route:** Climb up NW Skyline Blvd from NW Germantown Road, loop through the forest, and return via Newberry Road.
- **Key Roads:** NW Skyline Blvd, Newberry Road
- **Best For:** Hill repeat training, building climbing strength, race prep
- **⚠️ Notes:** Steep climbs — bring water and watch for traffic on Skyline Blvd. Not recommended for beginners.

| Detail | Value |
|--------|-------|

| **Elevation Gain** | ~250 ft (est.) |
| **Terrain** | Paved road (NW Saint Helens Road, NW Yeon Avenue) |
| **Est. Time** | 8–10 minutes at moderate pace |
