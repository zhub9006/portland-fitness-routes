## Route Description

A cycling loop through the scenic heart of Forest Park, following the same path as the walking loop optimized for bikes. The paved roads of NW Saint Helens Road, NW 25th Avenue, and NW Cornell Road make for a pleasant ride through old-growth forest. Ideal for warm-up loops and interval training.

## Seasonal Notes

- **Spring:** Wet roads — lower tire pressure
- **Summer:** Cool temps, best riding season
- **Fall:** Leaf debris — watch for slick leaves
- **Winter:** Icy conditions on Cornell Road — studded tires

## Safety Tips

- Use lights and mirrors — these roads carry traffic
- NW Cornell has significant rush hour vehicle traffic
- Ring bell at trail/junction crossings
- Watch for pedestrians on narrow bridge segments
