# Forest Park → Germantown Walking Route

**Type:** Walking
**Difficulty:** Moderate (long distance with some elevation)
**Best Season:** Year-round (bring layers)

---

## Route: Forest Park Center → Germantown Road via St. Johns Bridge

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Distance** | 16,894 m (~10.5 mi) |
| **Duration** | ~210 min walking (~3.5 hrs) |
| **Elevation Gain** | ~400 ft (mostly north-to-south descent then climb) |
| **OSM Verified** | ✅ Foot routing via Saint Johns Bridge, NW Germantown Rd |

**Coordinates:**
- Forest Park Center: 45.536, -122.739
- St. Johns Bridge: 45.593, -122.747
- Germantown Rd start: 45.570, -122.769

**Route Segments:**
| Segment | Distance | Road |
|---------|----------|------|
| Forest Park Center → NW St. Helens Rd | ~2,500 m | NW Cornell Rd → NW St. Helens Rd |
| NW St. Helens Rd → St. Johns Bridge | ~6,709 m | NW St. Helens Rd → Bridge Ave → Lombard St |
| St. Johns Bridge → Germantown Rd | ~7,685 m | Bridge Ave → Germantown Rd, NW Ridge roads |

**Highlights:**
- 🌉 Cross the St. Johns Bridge — panoramic river views
- 🏘️ Walk through the historic Germantown neighborhood
- 🌲 Forest immersion on the southern and northern sections
- ☕ End at Germantown cafes for a post-walk treat

**Running Club Notes:**
- This is a long walk — pair it with a meetup at Forest Park and end at Germantown
- Best done as a point-to-point adventure with a car shuttle
- Eastbound (Park → City) is mostly downhill
- Westbound (City → Park) is the climbing direction
- Not recommended in icy winter conditions on the bridge
