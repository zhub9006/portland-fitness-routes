# Forest Park Fire Roads Circuit

## Route Overview
- **Distance:** ~15 miles
- **Elevation Gain:** ~800 ft
- **Terrain:** Gravel fire roads, some single-track
- **Difficulty:** 🟡 Moderate
- **Start:** Lower Macleay parking area
- **GPS Coordinates:** 45.5709, -122.7716

## Description
A circuit through the heart of Forest Park using the fire road network. Routes combine Leif Erikson Drive with fire roads like Newberry Road and Lower Macleay Road. Great for mountain bike training and building endurance.

## Turn-by-Turn Directions
1. Start at Lower Macleay parking area (NW 29th Ave & NW Upshur St)
2. Take Lower Macleay Road north into the park
3. Connect to Leif Erikson Drive
4. Follow Leif Erikson Drive north through the forest
5. At the junction, take Newberry Road northwest
6. Continue on Newberry Road for ~3 miles
7. Loop back via Lower Macleay Road to the start
8. Optional: extend the circuit via additional fire roads (Stonedale, Upper Macleay)

## Key Roads
- Leif Erikson Drive
- Newberry Road
- Lower Macleay Road

## Best For
- Mountain biking
- Gravel cycling
- Trail endurance training

## Notes
- Fire roads are gravel — wider tires recommended (32mm+)
- Some single-track sections — appropriate MTB skills needed
- Weekday mornings are quietest
- Watch for other cyclists and hikers on narrow sections
