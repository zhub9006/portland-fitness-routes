# Route: Forest Park to NW Skyline Blvd Walk

- **Type:** Walking / Light Jog
- **Distance:** 9,735 meters (~6.1 miles)
- **Duration:** ~90 minutes walking / ~15 minutes running
- **Difficulty:** Easy to Moderate
- **Start:** Forest Park Nature Reserve area (45.571, -122.772)
- **End:** NW Skyline Boulevard area (45.547, -122.768)
- **Source:** OpenStreetMap routing analysis

## Description
A scenic walking route from the interior of Forest Park up through the park's fire roads and out to NW Skyline Boulevard. The route follows NW Saint Helens Road and NW Germantown Road through the old-growth forest canopy before emerging onto the paved Skyline Boulevard corridor. Great for a morning group walk or an easy trail-to-road transition run.

## Turn-by-Turn Directions (from OSM)
1. **Start** at Forest Park interior (trailhead near NW Engleman St / NW Saint Helens Rd)
2. Head southwest on **NW Saint Helens Road** — 1,776 m
3. Continue briefly on **NW Bridge Avenue** — 174 m
4. Turn onto **NW Germantown Road** — 3,356 m
5. Continue uphill on **NW Skyline Boulevard** — 4,112 m
6. Turn onto **NW Chapin Drive** — 59 m
7. End at **NW Herrin Court** — 102 m

## Key Features
- Old-growth forest canopy throughout
- NW Germantown Road is the signature climb
- Views of the Willamette Valley from Skyline Blvd
- Accessible trail surface for walking
- Connects to the Wildwood Trail network

## Facilities Nearby
- **Leif Erikson Drive** — Car-free paved road (weekdays dawn–10am, all day Sundays) for additional route options
- **Stevens Pavilion** (Stevens South) — Picnic shelter at SW 19th & SW Kingston Ave
- **Drinking water** available at multiple trailhead locations
- **Parking** at NW 29th Ave & Skyline Blvd trailhead lot
