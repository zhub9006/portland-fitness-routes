## Description
A popular cycling route connecting Forest Park to the North Portland waterfront. Follows major roads through the St. Johns area with a scenic crossing over the Willamette River via the historic St. Johns Bridge.

## Recommendations
- **Best For:** Experienced riders, distance training
- **Bring:** Water, phone, ID
- **Caution:** St. Johns Bridge has a narrow shared lane — be visible
- **Traffic:** North Portland Road has moderate car traffic — stay alert
- **Scenery:** Great views of the Willamette River from St. Johns Bridge and Marine Drive

## Combined with Forest Park Trails
For a longer ride, start in Forest Park on the fire roads or Leif Erikson Drive, then connect to this route via Northwest Saint Helens Road for a 15-20 mile mixed-terrain ride.
