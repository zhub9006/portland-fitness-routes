# Forest Park → Downtown Walking Route 🥾🏙️

**Route Type:** Walking  
**Distance:** 4,952 m (short); 12,013 m (extended to downtown)  
**Elevation Gain:** ~500 ft descent to river  
**Estimated Duration:** 8 min (short); 17 min (extended)

## Overview

Walk from the heart of Forest Park through NW Portland's residential neighborhoods all the way down to the Willamette River waterfront in downtown Portland. This route showcases the city's best of both worlds: forest canopy and urban energy.

## Route Details

- **Start:** Forest Park / Leif Erikson Dr area
- **End:** Willamette River Waterfront (downtown)
- **Distance:** 4,952 m (short to NW 23rd) / 12,013 m (extended to river)
- **Difficulty:** Easy (mostly downhill)

### Turn-by-Turn Directions (Short)
1. Start at Leif Erikson Dr & Skidmore St
2. Head south on Leif Erikson Dr
3. Turn onto NW Cornell Road
4. Turn onto NW 23rd Place
5. End at NW 23rd Avenue

### Turn-by-Turn Directions (Extended to Downtown)
1. Start at Leif Erikson Dr & Skidmore St
2. Head south on Leif Erikson Dr
3. Turn onto NW Cornell Road
4. Continue onto NW Westover Road
5. Turn onto NW 25th Avenue
6. Continue south through residential streets
7. Follow NW Wardway Street / NW Nicolai Street
8. Turn onto NW Saint Helens Road
9. Continue to downtown / Willamette River area

### Highlights
- Forest canopy at the start
- Charming NW Portland neighborhoods
- Gradual descent toward the river
- Willamette River waterfront finish
