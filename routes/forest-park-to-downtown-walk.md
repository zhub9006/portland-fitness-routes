## Route Overview

## How to Use This Route

### Option 1: Short Walk (30 min)
1. Start at **Forest Park road network** (45.536, -122.738)
2. Walk west on **Northwest Cornell Road** → 1,977 m
3. Turn onto **Northwest Westover Road** → 301 m
4. Continue through to **Hermosa Boulevard** → 220 m
5. Turn around and retrace your steps

### Option 2: Extended Walk to Downtown (1.5 hrs)
1. Start at **Forest Park** (45.536, -122.738)
2. Follow the full 12 km route detailed above
3. Finish at **North Channel Avenue** near the river
4. **Recommended:** Grab a coffee at a downtown cafe before heading back

### Option 3: Out-and-Back via Different Routes
1. Walk out via **Cornell Road → Lovejoy → 24th Avenue** (go northbound)
2. Return via **Stadium Freeway → Going Street → Lagoon Avenue** (different streets)

## Route Notes

- The route is **out-and-back friendly** — many turn-around points available
- **Leash laws** apply — keep dogs on a leash in the park sections
- **Watch for traffic** on the Speedway and Going Street segments
- **Best in dry weather** — some side streets can be muddy after rain
- **Car-free option:** Use **Leif Erikson Drive** on Sundays for the ridgeline portion
