# Forest Park to Downtown Walking Route

A walking route from Forest Park through residential neighborhoods to Portland's downtown and Willamette River waterfront.

## Route Overview

- **Type:** Walking
- **Total Distance:** 4,952 m (short route); 12,013 m (extended to downtown)
- **Elevation Gain:** ~500 ft (descent to downtown)
- **Difficulty:** Moderate
- **Surface:** Mix of tree-lined residential streets, park paths, and urban walkways

---

## Route Description

This route descends from the cool, forested areas of Forest Park through residential NW Portland neighborhoods, eventually reaching the vibrant downtown and the Willamette River waterfront. It's a great way to experience Portland's neighborhoods from the treetops to the river.

### Turn-by-Turn Directions

1. Start at Forest Park (45.5315, -122.6818)
2. Head east on NW 12th Avenue (129 m)
3. Turn north on NW 12th Avenue then east onto NW Glisan Street (238 m)
4. Continue to NW 15th Avenue briefly, then enter Stadium Freeway area (196 m total)
5. Cross through the NW memorial area (731 m)
6. Follow the park path system westward (7,144 m)
   - Walk through the forested areas of Forest Park
   - Cross key trail junctions
7. Transition to NW Front Avenue heading south (4,299 m)
   - Main arterial through NW Portland neighborhoods
8. Continue on NW Saint Helens Road heading east (3,072 m)
9. Turn south along NW Naito Parkway (605 m)
   - Runs along the Willamette River
10. Turn east onto NW 9th Avenue (161 m)
11. Turn south on NW Northrup Street (109 m)
12. Arrive at downtown Portland / Willamette River waterfront (45.5314, -122.6818)

### Key Highlights
- 🌲 Forest Park entry — cool, shady, and peaceful
- 🏘️ Residential NW Portland — beautiful neighborhoods with historic homes
- 🌊 Downtown Portland — urban energy and Portland's core
- 🚢 Willamette River waterfront — walk along the river
- 📸 Great views and photo opportunities along the way

### Preparation Tips
- Wear comfortable walking shoes for the 11+ km distance
- Bring water and snacks — the full route takes ~2.5 hours
- The descent to downtown is steep in places
- Plan for a return trip or use the MAX/bus for the ride back
