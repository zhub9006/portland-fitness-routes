## Description

A longer route from the lower NW Portland neighborhood up through residential streets and into Forest Park. This route builds endurance and includes a steady climb as you approach the forest. Ideal for club training rides and long runs.

## Turn-by-Turn Directions (Bike)

1. **Start** — Lower NW Portland area (NW Germaine Ave & NW 16th St)
2. **Head north** on NW Germaine Ave
3. **Continue** through NW 23rd Ave
4. **Turn right** onto NW Thurman St
5. **Continue** on NW 29th Ave (gradual climb)
6. **Turn left** onto NW Tangier St
7. **Continue** onto NW Saint Helens Rd
8. **Arrive** at Forest Park trailhead

## Turn-by-Turn Directions (Run/Walk)

1. **Start** — Lower NW Portland area (NW Germaine Ave & NW 16th St)
2. **Head north** on NW Germaine Ave
3. **Continue** through NW 23rd Ave
4. **Turn right** onto NW Thurman St
5. **Continue** on NW 29th Ave (steady pace up the hill)
6. **Turn left** onto NW Tangier St
7. **Continue** onto NW Saint Helens Rd
8. **Arrive** at Forest Park trailhead

## Tips

- The climb from NW 16th to NW 29th is gradual but sustained
- Best attempted after warming up
- Hydrate before the climb — no water fountains on NW Thurman
