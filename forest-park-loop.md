## Description

A scenic loop through the heart of Forest Park, one of the largest urban forests in the United States. The route winds through towering Douglas firs and western red cedars with occasional views of the Willamette River. Great for a quick club warm-up or a solo recovery run.

## Turn-by-Turn Directions

1. **Start** — Forest Park visitor area (NW Germaine St & NW 29th Ave)
2. **Head north** on the Main Trail (paved, well-marked)
3. **Continue** through the forest, staying on marked trails
4. **Loop clockwise** around the central ridge
5. **Return** to the starting point via the lower trail section

## Amenities Nearby

- Parking at NW 29th Ave trailhead
- Restrooms at the visitor center
- No water fountains on the loop — bring your own

## Tips

- Trails can be muddy after rain — trail shoes recommended
- Watch for trail crossings and wildlife
- Best in morning before afternoon fog rolls in
