## Trail Information

### Wildwood Trail
- **Length:** ~31.5 miles (50.7 km) of single-track trail
- **Location:** Forest Park, Portland, OR
- **Bounding Box:** 45.5539°N – 45.5810°N, 122.7853°W – 122.7605°W
- **Type:** Hiking / Trail Running
- **Terrain:** Dirt single-track, some rocky sections, roots and mud possible
- **Features:** Old-growth forest, creek crossings, wildlife (deer, owls, raccoons)
- **Trailheads:**
  - Lower Macleay Trailhead (NW 29th Ave & NW Upshur St)
  - Upper Macleay Trailhead (NW 29th Ave at Skyline Blvd)
  - Shadowfjord Trailhead (NW 30th Ave & Upshur St)
  - Leif Erikson Drive entrance

### Leif Erikson Drive
- **Length:** ~11 miles (one way)
- **Type:** Car-free paved road (weekdays dawn to ~10am, all day Sundays)
- **Location:** Runs the full length of Forest Park
- **Terrain:** Paved, wide enough for cycling and running side by side
- **Best For:** Long rides, family rides, road running

### Saint Helens Road
- **Length:** Primary road through Forest Park corridor
- **Type:** Paved road with some traffic
- **Location:** NW Saint Helens Road through Forest Park
- **Notes:** Shaded by old-growth canopy; use caution at intersections with motor vehicle traffic

### Hoyt Arboretum
- **Length:** N/A (arboretum loop paths)
- **Location:** SW Berrywood Dr, Portland, OR (adjacent to Forest Park)
- **Features:** 2,000+ plant species from around the world, paved paths, visitor center
- **Best For:** Easy walking, nature education, pre-ride warm-up
