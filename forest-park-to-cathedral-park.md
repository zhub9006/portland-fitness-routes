## Description

A rewarding route from Forest Park down through Northwest Portland streets, culminating at Cathedral Park — a beautiful green space beneath the iconic Saint Johns Bridge. The climb from the neighborhood up to Forest Park is the main challenge; the descent on the return is faster.

## Turn-by-Turn Directions (Bike)

1. **Start** — Forest Park area (NW Germaine St & NW 29th Ave)
2. **Head south** on NW Germaine St
3. **Turn right** onto NW Tangier St
4. **Continue** onto NW Saint Helens Rd (rolling climb)
5. **Turn left** onto NW Bridge Ave
6. **Cross** the Saint Johns Bridge
7. **Turn right** onto N Philadelphia Ave
8. **Continue** on N Willamette Blvd
9. **Turn left** onto N Baltimore Ave
10. **Arrive** at Cathedral Park

## Turn-by-Turn Directions (Run/Walk)

1. **Start** — Forest Park area (NW Germaine St & NW 29th Ave)
2. **Head south** on NW Germaine St
3. **Turn right** onto NW Tangier St
4. **Continue** onto NW Saint Helens Rd (pace yourself on the climb)
5. **Turn left** onto NW Bridge Ave
6. **Cross** the Saint Johns Bridge
7. **Turn right** onto N Philadelphia Ave
8. **Continue** on N Willamette Blvd
9. **Turn left** onto N Baltimore Ave
10. **Arrive** at Cathedral Park

## Amenities Along Route

- Restrooms and parking at Cathedral Park
- Scenic views from the Saint Johns Bridge
- Off-street parking near the trailhead

## Tips

- The climb from NW 29th Ave to Forest Park is steep — great interval training!
- Saint Johns Bridge has wide sidewalks for pedestrians
- Cathedral Park has restrooms and picnic tables
